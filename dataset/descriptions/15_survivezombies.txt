**Player role:**
The player controls a character that moves in four directions using keyboard input. The character has health points and can collect resources.

**Entities:**
- Floor: Static ground tiles that form the base terrain
- Flower: A pink spawning point that occasionally creates bees with a low probability
- Fast hell: A red tombstone that frequently spawns zombies with moderate probability
- Slow hell: A red tombstone that spawns zombies less frequently than fast hell
- Honey: A golden collectible resource with a maximum limit of 10 that can be gathered
- Avatar: The player's character, a swordsman that can move in all directions and has health points
- Bee: A yellow creature that moves randomly around the map at normal speed with brief pauses between movements
- Zombie: A brown undead creature that actively chases the player character at half speed with longer pauses between movements
- Wall: Static barrier tiles that block movement and use connected textures

**Interactions:**
- When the avatar touches honey, it collects the resource and gains one point
- When the avatar touches honey, the honey disappears
- When any moving creature hits a wall, it bounces back and cannot pass through
- When the avatar encounters a zombie while having less than one honey, the avatar is destroyed and the score decreases by one
- When the avatar encounters a zombie, one honey is removed from the avatar's inventory
- When a zombie touches the avatar, the avatar is destroyed
- When a bee encounters a zombie, the bee transforms into honey
- When a zombie touches a bee, the bee is destroyed
- When the avatar steps on any hell spawn point, the avatar is destroyed

**Win condition:**
The player wins if they survive for the full time limit of 1000 game steps.

**Lose condition:**
The player loses if their avatar is destroyed.

**Objective:**
Survive as long as possible while collecting honey to protect yourself from zombies. Avoid spawn points and use honey strategically since you need it to survive zombie encounters.
