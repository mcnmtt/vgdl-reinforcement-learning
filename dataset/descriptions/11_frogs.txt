**Player role:**
The player controls a character that can move in all four directions using keyboard input. The character moves one step at a time and cannot move through walls or off the edge of the screen - attempting to do so causes the character to stay in place.

**Entities:**
- **Character**: The player's avatar that can move around the game area
- **Goal**: A green exit point that the player must reach to win
- **Water**: Blue water areas that are dangerous - stepping on them reduces the player's safety and can kill them if they don't have enough safety points
- **Logs**: Brown objects that float leftward slowly and provide safety when the player steps on them; they also carry the player along as they move
- **Fast orange trucks**: Orange vehicles that move quickly either left or right depending on their type
- **Slow red trucks**: Red vehicles that move slowly either left or right depending on their type  
- **Walls**: Black tree-like barriers that block movement
- **Dense forest spawners**: Areas that frequently create new logs (40% chance every 10 time units)
- **Sparse forest spawners**: Areas that occasionally create new logs (10% chance every 5 time units)
- **Grass**: Hidden ground terrain
- **Highway**: Hidden road terrain
- **Safety**: An invisible resource that tracks how protected the player is

**Interactions:**
- When the character reaches the goal, the character disappears and the player gains one point
- When the character steps on a log, the character gains 2 safety points
- When the character steps on a log, the character is pulled along with the log as it moves
- When the character tries to move into a wall or off the edge of the screen, the character steps back to its previous position
- When the character enters water with less than 0 safety points, the character is destroyed
- When the character enters water, the character loses 1 safety point
- When the character touches any truck, the character is destroyed
- When logs reach the edge of the screen, they disappear
- When trucks reach the edge of the screen, they wrap around to the opposite side

**Win condition:**
The player wins when they reach the goal (when there are no more goal sprites remaining on the screen).

**Lose condition:**
The player loses if their character is destroyed by trucks or by entering water without sufficient safety points.

**Objective:**
Navigate safely across dangerous roads filled with moving trucks and through water areas by riding on logs to maintain your safety, ultimately reaching the green exit goal.
