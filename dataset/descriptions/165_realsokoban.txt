Player role:
The player controls a knight character that can move around the game area. The knight moves normally but cannot pass through walls - when it tries to move into a wall, it bounces back to its previous position.

Entities:
- Knight: The player-controlled character that can move around the level
- Walls: Immovable barriers that block movement and have decorative tiling that connects adjacent wall pieces
- Boxes: Movable objects that the player can push around the level
- Boxes in holes: Boxes that have fallen into holes and appear with a different visual style
- Holes: Dark blue pits or depressions in the ground where boxes can be placed
- Floor areas: Hidden ground tiles that provide the basic walking surface
- Ground areas: The standard walkable surface of the level

Interactions:
- When the knight tries to move into a wall, the knight bounces back to where it was before
- When the knight touches a box, the box gets pushed forward in the direction the knight was moving
- When a box gets pushed against a wall while another box is also involved, all boxes return to their previous positions
- When a box moves onto a hole, the box transforms into a box-in-hole and the player gains 1 point
- When a box-in-hole moves onto regular ground, it transforms back into a regular box and the player loses 1 point

Win condition:
The player wins when there are no regular boxes left on the level (meaning all boxes have been successfully placed in holes).

Lose condition:
There is no way for the player to lose this game.

Objective:
Push all the boxes into holes to complete the level. Boxes that fall into holes stay there and increase your score, while removing them decreases your score.
