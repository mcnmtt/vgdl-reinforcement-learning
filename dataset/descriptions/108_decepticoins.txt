**Player role:**
The player controls a knight character that can move in four directions (up, down, left, right) using keyboard input.

**Entities:**
- Floor tiles: Basic ground surfaces that form the walkable area of the level
- Holes: Dark blue pits in the ground that destroy objects that fall into them
- Boxes: Movable brown blocks that the player can push around the level
- Walls: Immovable barriers that block movement and automatically connect to adjacent walls
- Coins: Golden collectible items that remain stationary
- Switches: Special floor tiles that trigger effects when stepped on
- Knight (avatar): The player-controlled character that moves around the level
- Piranhas: Enemies that chase the player character, moving toward the player's position but only taking action every few moments

**Interactions:**
- When any moving object hits a wall, it stops and cannot move through
- When the knight touches a piranha, the knight is destroyed
- When a box is pushed by the knight, the box slides forward in the direction of the push
- When a box hits a wall or another box, all recent box movements are reversed
- When a box falls into a hole, the box is destroyed and the player gains 1 point
- When the knight collects a coin, the coin disappears and the player gains 1 point
- When the knight steps on a switch, a wall appears behind the knight and the switch disappears

**Win condition:**
The player wins when all boxes have been removed from the level (typically by pushing them into holes).

**Lose condition:**
The player loses if the knight is destroyed by touching a piranha.

**Objective:**
Push all the boxes into holes to clear them from the level while avoiding the chasing piranhas and collecting coins for extra points.
