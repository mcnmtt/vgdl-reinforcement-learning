**Player role:**
The player controls a character that can move in four directions and shoot water projectiles. The character cannot move through walls or off the edge of the screen - it will bounce back if it tries to do so.

**Entities:**
- Floor: An invisible surface that serves as the ground throughout the level
- Player character: A animated figure that moves around and shoots water
- Plant starter: An invisible placeholder that automatically becomes a fresh plant after a moment
- Fresh plant: A healthy green bush that slowly begins to wilt over time
- Wilting plant: A plant in decline that will become wilted if not watered soon
- Wilted plant: A severely declining plant that will die if not revived with water
- Dead plant: A red plant that has died completely
- Overwatered plant: A blue plant that has received too much water and died from overwatering
- Water projectile: A flickering blue droplet that the player shoots, with only one able to exist at a time
- Wall: A black barrier that blocks movement

**Interactions:**
- When the player character touches a wall or reaches the edge of the screen, it bounces back to its previous position
- Plant starters automatically transform into fresh plants after a brief moment
- Fresh plants automatically begin wilting after 100 time units
- Wilting plants automatically become wilted after 160 time units  
- Wilted plants automatically die and turn red after 210 time units, reducing the score by 10 points
- When water hits a fresh plant, the plant becomes overwatered and blue, the score decreases by 5 points, and the water disappears
- When water hits a wilting plant, it becomes fresh and healthy again, the score increases by 5 points, and the water disappears
- When water hits a wilted plant, it improves to wilting status, the score increases by 2 points, and the water disappears

**Win condition:**
The player wins by surviving for 1500 time units without any plants dying or becoming overwatered.

**Lose condition:**
The player loses immediately if any plant dies (turns red) or becomes overwatered (turns blue).

**Objective:**
Keep all plants alive and healthy by watering them at the right time - water wilting and wilted plants to revive them, but avoid overwatering fresh plants.
