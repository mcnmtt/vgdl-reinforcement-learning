**Player role:**
The player controls a swordsman who can move in four directions and shoot a sword that briefly appears and disappears. The swordsman changes appearance when carrying a key, showing an orange-colored version to indicate the key is held.

**Entities:**
- Floor tiles that form the walkable surface of the game world
- A small green door that serves as one type of goal
- A large gold door that serves as another type of goal  
- An orange key that can be picked up by the player
- A red vase that can be destroyed for points
- A sword projectile that flickers briefly when shot, with a maximum of 5 uses
- The player's swordsman character, which appears as a regular swordsman or an orange swordsman when holding a key
- A quick bat enemy that moves randomly and changes direction every 2 time units
- A normal spider enemy that moves randomly and changes direction every 4 time units  
- A slow scorpion enemy that moves randomly and changes direction every 8 time units
- Walls that block movement and form barriers in the level

**Interactions:**
- All moving objects bounce back when they hit walls
- The swordsman without a key bounces back when trying to enter the small green door
- The small green door disappears when the swordsman with a key enters it, awarding 1 point
- The large gold door disappears when any version of the swordsman enters it, awarding 10 points
- Enemies are destroyed when hit by the sword, awarding 2 points each
- Vases are destroyed when hit by the sword, awarding 1 point
- Enemies bounce back when they collide with each other
- The swordsman is destroyed when touched by an enemy, losing 1 point
- When the swordsman without a key touches the key, he transforms into the orange key-holding version, the key disappears, and 1 point is awarded

**Win condition:**
The player wins when all small green doors and large gold doors have been entered and destroyed.

**Lose condition:**
The player loses if the swordsman is destroyed by touching an enemy.

**Objective:**
Guide the swordsman through the level to collect the key and enter all doors while avoiding or destroying enemies with the sword to maximize your score.
