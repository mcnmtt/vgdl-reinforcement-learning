Player role:
The player controls a character that can move in all directions using arrow keys or similar controls. The character moves freely across the game area without any special physics like gravity or momentum.

Entities:
- A character (appears as a girl) that the player directly controls and can move in any direction
- A green exit door that serves as the goal destination for the level
- Walls that block movement and cannot be passed through
- Spike traps that are dangerous obstacles placed on the ground
- A grass floor that covers the entire play area as the background surface

Interactions:
- When the character tries to move into a wall, it is pushed back and cannot pass through
- When the character reaches the exit door, the character disappears and the player gains one point
- When the character touches a spike trap, the character is destroyed and the player loses one point

Win condition:
The player wins when all exit doors have been reached and used (typically there is one exit per level).

Lose condition:
The player loses if the character is destroyed by touching a trap, or if the time limit of 1000 game steps is reached.

Objective:
Navigate the character through the level while avoiding deadly spike traps and reach the green exit door to complete the level successfully.
