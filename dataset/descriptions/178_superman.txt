Player role:
The player controls a superman character that can move in all directions using arrow keys or similar controls. The superman moves on streets and can fly through the sky areas of the game world.

Entities:
- Superman: The player's character that can move freely around the game world
- Sky: Background areas that characters can move through
- Buildings: Solid structures that block movement and provide platforms for civilians
- Streets: Ground-level areas where the superman moves
- Empty spaces: Invisible areas that represent destroyed parts of buildings
- Jail: A structure where captured villains are deposited
- Portal doors: Special doors that continuously spawn bandits - there are left-facing and right-facing versions that produce bandits moving in their respective directions
- Bandits: Enemy characters that move horizontally at medium speed, bounce off walls, and occasionally fire missiles upward
- Missiles: Fast-moving projectiles fired by bandits that travel upward
- Standing civilians: People who stand on buildings and fall down when buildings beneath them are destroyed
- Falling civilians: Civilians who are dropping down after losing their footing
- Hurt civilians: Civilians who have been injured by hitting walls while falling
- Walls: Solid barriers that block movement
- Villain counter: An invisible counter that tracks how many bandits the player has caught
- Villain gang counter: An invisible counter used when depositing a full group of villains at the jail

Interactions:
- When superman touches walls, the edge of the screen, or portals, he cannot pass through and is pushed back
- When bandits hit walls, they reverse direction and are pushed back
- When missiles reach the edge of the screen, they disappear
- When missiles hit buildings, the building transforms into an empty space and the missile is destroyed
- When standing civilians touch buildings, they cannot pass through
- When standing civilians touch empty spaces, they transform into falling civilians
- When falling civilians touch buildings, they transform back into standing civilians
- When falling civilians hit walls, they transform into hurt civilians
- When falling civilians touch superman, they disappear and the player gains 1 point
- When superman touches bandits, the villain counter increases by 1, the bandit disappears, and the player gains 1 point
- When superman touches the jail while having 8 or more villains, a villain gang marker appears at the jail and 8 villains are removed from the counter
- When the villain gang marker touches the jail, it disappears and the player gains 1000 points

Win condition:
The player wins when all portals and bandits have been eliminated from the game.

Lose condition:
The player loses if any civilian becomes hurt, or if superman is destroyed.

Objective:
Guide superman to capture bandits and deposit them at the jail while protecting civilians from falling and getting hurt when buildings are destroyed by bandit missiles.
