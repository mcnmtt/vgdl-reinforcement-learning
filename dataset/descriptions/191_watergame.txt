**Player role:**
The player controls a mage character that can move in all four directions using keyboard input. The mage moves one step at a time when direction keys are pressed.

**Entities:**
- Floor tiles that form the background of the level and can be walked on
- A mage (the player character) that moves around the level
- Walls that block movement and appear as solid barriers
- Water areas that are dangerous to touch
- Boxes (appearing as potions) that can be pushed around the level
- Doors that the player needs to interact with

**Interactions:**
- When the mage tries to move into a wall, it is pushed back to its previous position
- When the mage moves into a box, the box is pushed forward in the same direction
- When a box is pushed into a wall or into another box, all affected objects return to their previous positions
- When a box touches water, both the box and water disappear
- When the mage touches water, the mage is destroyed
- When the mage touches a door, the door disappears

**Win condition:**
The player wins when all doors have been removed from the level.

**Lose condition:**
The player loses if the mage is destroyed (by touching water).

**Objective:**
Guide the mage through the level to touch and remove all doors while avoiding water and using boxes strategically to help navigate safely.
