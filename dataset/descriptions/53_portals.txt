Player role:
Two players each control a character that can move in four directions and shoot portal projectiles. One player controls a blue character that shoots blue portal projectiles, and the other controls an orange character that shoots orange portal projectiles. Both characters are affected by solid obstacles and boundaries.

Entities:
- Floor: Hidden surface that provides the ground level
- Wall: Solid barriers that block movement and projectiles
- Trap: Deadly hazards that destroy characters on contact
- Cake: A target object that destroys any character that touches it
- Laser: Projectiles fired by turrets that travel in straight lines until hitting obstacles
- Blue portal projectile: A projectile shot by the blue character that creates blue portals on walls
- Orange portal projectile: A projectile shot by the orange character that creates orange portals on walls
- Blue portal (disabled): An inactive blue portal that appears as a blue wall section
- Orange portal (disabled): An inactive orange portal that appears as an orange wall section  
- Blue portal (working): An active blue portal that teleports characters to the orange portal
- Orange portal (working): An active orange portal that teleports characters to the blue portal
- Blue character (normal): A blue character that can move and shoot blue portal projectiles
- Orange character (normal): An orange character that can move and shoot orange portal projectiles
- Blue character (no shooting): A blue character that can only move, cannot shoot
- Orange character (no shooting): An orange character that can only move, cannot shoot
- Turret: A stationary enemy that shoots laser projectiles at regular intervals

Interactions:
- When any movable object hits a wall or the edge of the screen, it is pushed back to its previous position
- When a laser hits a wall, the laser is destroyed
- When a turret encounters cake or any portal, the turret is pushed back
- When any character touches a laser, trap, or cake, the character is destroyed
- When a portal projectile hits the edge of the screen, it is destroyed
- When a shooting character touches a working portal, they are teleported to the corresponding portal
- When a blue shooting character touches a working portal, they become unable to shoot for a brief period
- When an orange shooting character touches a working portal, they become unable to shoot for a brief period
- When a blue portal projectile hits a wall, the blue character transforms into the orange character, the projectile is destroyed, the wall transforms into a blue working portal, and all existing blue portals are destroyed
- When an orange portal projectile hits a wall, the orange character transforms into the blue character, the projectile is destroyed, the wall transforms into an orange working portal, and all existing orange portals are destroyed

Win condition:
Player 1 wins when all cakes are destroyed and Player 2's character is eliminated. Player 2 wins when all cakes are destroyed and Player 1's character is eliminated.

Lose condition:
A player loses when their character is destroyed while the other player's character survives.

Objective:
Work together or compete to destroy the cake while avoiding traps, lasers, and other hazards, using portal projectiles to create teleportation links between walls for strategic movement.
