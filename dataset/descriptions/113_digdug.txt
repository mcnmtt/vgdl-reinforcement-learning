**Player role:**
The player controls a character that can move in four directions and shoot shovels. The character moves freely without being affected by gravity or other physics.

**Entities:**
- Floor: An invisible surface that covers most of the game area
- Gold coins: Small golden collectible items that remain stationary
- Green gems: Small green diamond-shaped collectible items that remain stationary
- Shovels: Gray pickaxe tools that flicker briefly when shot, lasting only a short time before disappearing
- Resting weapon: A stationary round object that doesn't move
- Boulder: A round projectile that moves slowly forward
- Player character: The main character that can move and shoot shovels
- Red monster: A red scorpion-like enemy that moves downward with brief pauses between movements
- Falling object: A yellow round object that falls downward at moderate speed
- Monster entrance: A hole that periodically spawns up to 5 monsters with long intervals between spawns
- Walls: Brown dirt barriers that block movement and interact with various objects

**Interactions:**
- When a boulder hits gold, the gold transforms into a falling object
- When the player touches a gem, the gem disappears and the player gains 1 point
- When a boulder hits a wall, the boulder becomes a resting weapon
- When a resting weapon touches a wall, it bounces back
- When the player touches gold, the player bounces back without collecting it
- When the player touches a monster, the player is destroyed and loses 1 point
- When a monster hits a wall, the edge of the screen, or a resting weapon, it reverses direction
- When a monster touches a boulder, the monster is destroyed and the player gains 2 points
- When a falling object hits a wall, it disappears
- When two shovels collide, they transform into a boulder
- When a boulder or resting weapon reaches the edge of the screen, it bounces back
- When a boulder or resting weapon touches the player, the player is destroyed
- When a shovel hits a wall, the shovel disappears
- When any moving object hits a wall, the edge of the screen, or a resting weapon, it bounces back
- When any moving object touches a falling object, the moving object is destroyed

**Win condition:**
The player wins when all gems and gold are collected AND all monsters are eliminated.

**Lose condition:**
The player loses when their character is destroyed.

**Objective:**
Collect all gems while avoiding monsters and managing the dangerous boulders created by combining shovels. Clear all monsters from the level to achieve victory.
