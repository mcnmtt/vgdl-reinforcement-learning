**Player role:**
You control a character that can move around and shoot sparks. The character bounces back when trying to move into walls or off the edge of the screen.

**Entities:**
- Floor tiles that provide the basic ground surface (invisible background elements)
- Ground tiles that serve as a foundation layer (invisible background elements)
- Your character avatar that can move and shoot orange sparks
- Traps that are deadly to touch, appearing as floor spaces
- Hidden bridge sections that are initially invisible and become visible wooden dock pieces when illuminated
- An exit door that ends the level when reached
- Orange sparks that you shoot, which spread light and flicker with illumination effects
- Black walls that block movement and auto-connect to form continuous barriers

**Interactions:**
- When your character touches a wall or reaches the edge of the screen, it bounces back and cannot pass through
- When light touches a hidden bridge section, the bridge becomes visible as a wooden dock
- When a trap touches a visible bridge, the trap is destroyed
- When your character touches a trap, your character is destroyed
- When your character touches the exit, the exit is destroyed (completing the level)

**Win condition:**
You win by reaching the exit door, or if the timer runs out after 1500 time units.

**Lose condition:**
You lose if your character is destroyed by touching a trap.

**Objective:**
Navigate through the level by shooting sparks to illuminate hidden bridges, which will destroy traps and create safe passages to reach the exit door.
