Player role:
This is a two-player cooperative game. Player 1 controls a wheelchair user who moves in four directions and has health points that slowly decrease over time. Player 2 controls a caregiver who can move in four directions and can activate objects by shooting or using them.

Entities:
- Floor: White street-like surface that characters can walk on
- Gap: Grass-textured holes in the ground that block the wheelchair
- Wall: Tree-like barriers that block all characters
- Ramp: Log-like objects that can be placed to cover gaps
- Charging point: Laser cannon-like stations that restore and increase the wheelchair's maximum health
- Wheelchair user: A guard-like character with 50 health points that gradually lose health over time
- Caregiver: An ogre-like character who can activate objects
- Use action: A flickering belt-like effect that appears when the caregiver activates something
- Hostile person: A man who chases the wheelchair user at slow speed
- Exit door: The goal location that ends the level
- Invisible portals: Hidden teleportation objects that move characters to starting positions

Interactions:
- All characters are pushed back when they hit walls or the edge of the screen
- The wheelchair is pushed back when it tries to enter gaps
- The hostile person and caregiver are pushed back when they hit walls
- The caregiver is pushed back when touching charging points
- When the wheelchair touches a hostile person, both are teleported back to their starting positions
- When the caregiver uses their ability on a gap, a ramp appears and covers the gap
- When the wheelchair reaches a charging point, its health is restored and maximum health increases by 10 points
- When the wheelchair reaches the exit door, it disappears from that location
- The wheelchair loses health points automatically every few seconds

Win condition:
Both players win when the wheelchair user reaches the exit door.

Lose condition:
Both players lose if the wheelchair user's health reaches zero.

Objective:
Work together to help the wheelchair user navigate obstacles and reach the exit while managing their declining health and avoiding hostile characters.
