**Player role:**
The player controls a character that can turn and move continuously in the direction it's facing at a steady pace. The character can rotate to face different directions and moves forward automatically.

**Entities:**
- Floor: Hidden background surface that covers the playing area
- Player walls: Immovable wall barriers that the player can create or that serve as obstacles
- Avatar: The player character that moves forward continuously and can turn to change direction
- Garbage: Stationary objects scattered throughout the level that the player needs to collect
- Walls: Immovable barrier walls that automatically connect to adjacent walls for seamless appearance

**Interactions:**
- When the avatar touches player walls or regular walls, the avatar is destroyed and the player loses 1 point
- When the avatar touches garbage, the garbage is destroyed and the player gains 2 points

**Win condition:**
The player wins when all garbage has been collected (no garbage remains on the screen).

**Lose condition:**
The player loses if the avatar is destroyed by hitting a wall, or if the time limit of 1000 time units expires.

**Objective:**
Navigate your character through the level to collect all the garbage while avoiding walls and player-created barriers before time runs out.
