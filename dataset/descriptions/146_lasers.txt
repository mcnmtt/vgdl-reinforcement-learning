Player role:
The player controls an archer that can rotate in place to face different directions and shoot blue orbs. The archer moves in discrete steps and cannot pass through solid objects.

Entities:
- An archer (the player character) that can rotate to face any direction and shoot blue orbs
- A goal door that serves as the exit point for the level
- Movable boxes that can be pushed around
- Laser cannons that come in different orientations - some point up, down, left, or right, and one special cannon that can change direction
- Yellow laser beams that move slowly in straight lines from the cannons toward their facing direction
- Blue orbs that the player shoots, which are limited to one at a time and move in the direction the archer was facing when fired
- Walls that block movement and destroy projectiles
- Floor tiles that provide the ground surface

Interactions:
- When the archer touches a wall or laser cannon, it steps back to its previous position
- When the archer touches a box from behind, the box gets pushed forward and the archer moves into the box's previous space
- When a box would move into a wall, another box, or laser cannon, the movement is prevented entirely
- When the archer touches a laser beam head-on, the archer is destroyed
- When the archer touches a laser beam from any other angle, the archer is destroyed
- When the archer is behind a box and a laser beam hits the box from the front, the box protects the archer from destruction
- When laser beams hit walls or boxes, the beams are destroyed
- When blue orbs hit walls, laser cannons, or reach the edge of the screen, they are destroyed
- When the archer reaches the goal door, the archer disappears and the player scores 5 points
- When a blue orb hits a laser cannon pointing down, that cannon transforms into the special rotating cannon
- When a blue orb hits a laser cannon pointing left, it transforms into a downward-pointing cannon
- When a blue orb hits a laser cannon pointing up, it transforms into a leftward-pointing cannon
- When a blue orb hits a laser cannon pointing right, it transforms into an upward-pointing cannon
- When a blue orb hits the special rotating cannon, it transforms into a rightward-pointing cannon

Win condition:
The player wins when there are no goal doors remaining (meaning the archer successfully reached the exit).

Lose condition:
The player loses when the archer is destroyed by touching a laser beam.

Objective:
Navigate the archer through the level by pushing boxes and shooting orbs to rotate laser cannons, creating a safe path to reach the goal door while avoiding the deadly laser beams.
