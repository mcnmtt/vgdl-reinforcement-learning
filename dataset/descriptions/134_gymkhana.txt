**Player role:**
The player controls a character that can move in four directions around the game area. The player can switch between two modes: a walking mode where they move normally on the ground, and a projectile mode where they shoot themselves in one of four directions (north, south, east, or west) with different colored appearances for each direction.

**Entities:**
- **Water**: Blue areas that are deadly to touch when walking
- **Floor**: Hidden ground tiles that provide safe walking surfaces
- **Walls**: Bush-like obstacles that block movement and destroy logs
- **Goal**: A green door that the player must reach to win
- **Forest spawn points**: Areas that periodically create logs moving in specific directions (right or left)
- **Logs**: Brown objects that move horizontally at slow speed, either leftward or rightward depending on their type
- **Landing pads**: Stationary orange platforms that can carry the walking player
- **Catapults**: Directional devices that face north, south, east, or west, colored red, orange, or yellow respectively, and launch the player as a projectile
- **Walking avatar**: The player's normal form that can move on ground and logs
- **Projectile avatar**: The player's bullet form that flies in the aimed direction, appearing as different colors based on direction

**Interactions:**
- When a projectile hits a wall or log, the player transforms back into walking mode
- When the walking player tries to move into a wall, they are pushed back with precise collision detection
- When the walking player stands on a log, they are protected from water and move along with the log
- When the walking player stands on a landing pad, they move with it using precise positioning
- When a landing pad touches the walking player, the landing pad is destroyed
- When a log hits a wall, the log is destroyed
- When any form of the player touches a catapult, they are aligned with it and face the same direction
- When the player touches a west-facing catapult, they become a westward projectile
- When the player touches an east-facing catapult, they become an eastward projectile
- When the player touches a south-facing catapult, they become a southward projectile
- When the player touches a north-facing catapult, they become a northward projectile
- When a catapult touches the player, the player is destroyed and the score increases by 1
- When the goal touches the player, the player is destroyed (completing the level)
- When the walking player touches water, they are destroyed
- When the player tries to enter a forest spawn point, they are pushed back

**Win condition:**
The player wins when they reach the goal (when there are no more goal sprites remaining on the field).

**Lose condition:**
The player loses if they are destroyed by water, catapults, or if the time limit of 1000 time units expires.

**Objective:**
Navigate safely across water-filled areas by riding on moving logs and using catapults to launch yourself, while avoiding deadly water and obstacles, to reach the green goal door before time runs out.
