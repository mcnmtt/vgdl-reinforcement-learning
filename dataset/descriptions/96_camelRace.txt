**Player role:**
The player controls a camel that can move in four directions using keyboard input. The camel moves at a normal speed and can navigate freely around the game area.

**Entities:**
- Floor: An invisible surface that covers the playable area with a beige background
- Player's camel: A controllable camel that faces left or right depending on movement direction
- Random camel: A brown camel that moves randomly in all directions at a slow pace, changing direction frequently
- Fast camels: Brown camels that move quickly in straight lines, either left or right, never changing direction
- Medium camels: Brown camels that move at moderate speed in straight lines, either left or right, never changing direction  
- Slow camels: Brown camels that move slowly in straight lines, either left or right, never changing direction
- Goal: A green exit door that serves as the finish line
- Winner markers: Special sparkle effects that appear when someone reaches the goal - pink for the player, red for other camels
- Walls: Immovable brown dirt barriers that block movement

**Interactions:**
- When the player's camel reaches the goal, it transforms into a pink sparkle effect and the player gains one point
- When any other camel reaches the goal, it transforms into a red sparkle effect and the player loses one point
- When the player's camel hits a wall, it bounces back and cannot pass through
- When any other camel hits a wall, it bounces back and cannot pass through

**Win condition:**
The player wins when their camel reaches the goal first, creating a pink sparkle effect.

**Lose condition:**
The player loses if any of the other camels reaches the goal before them, creating a red sparkle effect.

**Objective:**
Race against the other camels to be the first to reach the green goal door while navigating around walls and obstacles.
