**Player role:**
The player controls a yellow spaceship that can move in all directions and shoot blue bullets. The spaceship cannot rotate - it always faces the same direction. The spaceship has limited ammunition that can be replenished by collecting supplies.

**Entities:**
- **Yellow spaceship (avatar):** The player's ship that can move and shoot blue bullets upward
- **White cities:** Stationary structures that must be protected from enemy attacks
- **Blue aliens:** Enemy ships that move leftward across the screen and occasionally drop red bombs downward
- **Blue bullets:** Projectiles fired by the player's spaceship that travel rightward
- **Red bombs:** Explosive projectiles dropped by aliens that fall downward at moderate speed
- **Gold supply drops:** Items that fall slowly downward and provide ammunition when collected
- **Invisible alien spawners:** Hidden points that periodically create new aliens (some spawn aliens more frequently than others)
- **Invisible supply spawners:** Hidden points that occasionally create ammunition supply drops
- **Walls:** Immovable barriers that block movement
- **Invisible floor:** Hidden boundary surface throughout the level

**Interactions:**
- When the spaceship hits the edge of the screen, a city, or a wall, it is pushed back and cannot pass through
- When an alien reaches the edge of the screen, it disappears
- When any projectile (bullet or bomb) hits the edge of the screen or a city, it disappears
- When a bomb hits a city, both the bomb and city are destroyed, and the player loses 1 point
- When a bullet or alien touches a city, both are destroyed
- When a supply drop touches an alien, both are destroyed
- When a bullet hits an alien, both are destroyed and the player gains 2 points
- When supply drops collide with each other, they bounce away from each other
- When a supply drop hits a wall or city, it bounces back
- When the spaceship collects a supply drop, the player gains 5 units of ammunition and the supply disappears

**Win condition:**
The player wins by surviving for 1000 time units without losing all spaceships or cities.

**Lose condition:**
The player loses if their spaceship is destroyed or if all cities are destroyed by enemy bombs.

**Objective:**
Defend the cities from alien bombers by shooting them down while collecting ammunition supplies to maintain your ability to fight, and survive long enough to achieve victory.
