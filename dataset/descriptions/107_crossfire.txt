Player role:
The player controls a knight that can move in all directions using keyboard input.

Entities:
- Floor: Hidden background tiles that cover the game area
- Knight (avatar): The player-controlled character that moves around the level
- Turret: A stationary cannon that rotates in place and randomly shoots bombs with a 40% chance every 3 time units at slow speed
- Bomb: Red projectiles fired by turrets that move quickly across the screen and appear smaller than normal size
- Goal: A green open door that the player must reach
- Wall: Immutable barrier blocks that automatically connect to adjacent walls for seamless appearance

Interactions:
- When the knight reaches a goal, the goal disappears and the player gains 5 points
- When the knight hits a wall, the knight is pushed back and cannot pass through
- When a turret hits a wall, the turret is pushed back
- When the knight is hit by a bomb, the knight is destroyed and the player loses 1 point
- When a bomb hits a wall, the bomb is destroyed

Win condition:
The player wins when all goals have been collected (when there are no goals remaining on the level).

Lose condition:
The player loses when the knight is destroyed by a bomb (when there are no avatars remaining on the level).

Objective:
Navigate the knight through the level to collect all the green doors while avoiding the red bombs shot by rotating turrets.
