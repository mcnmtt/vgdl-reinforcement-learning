Player role:
You control a yellow spaceship that moves in all directions at moderate speed. The spaceship can move freely around the playable area.

Entities:
- A yellow spaceship (your character) that moves at moderate speed in any direction
- Gray floor tiles that form the background of safe areas
- Blue water-like enemy territory where hostile creatures move
- Small blue diamonds scattered throughout the level that can be collected
- Larger red diamonds that are special collectible items with enhanced properties
- Sparkling items that flicker rapidly and disappear after a short time
- Dark robed enemies that move horizontally - some move rightward along the top areas, others move leftward along the bottom areas
- Projectiles fired upward from bottom enemies, appearing as upward-pointing flames
- Projectiles fired downward from top enemies, appearing as downward-pointing flames
- Green diamond-shaped resources that track your collection progress
- Gray walls that block movement and form boundaries

Interactions:
- When your spaceship touches walls, enemy territory, enemies, or their projectiles, it is destroyed and you lose 10 points
- When your spaceship touches small blue diamonds, the diamonds disappear and you gain 1 point
- When your spaceship touches large red diamonds, they transform into sparkling flickering items
- When sparkling items touch your spaceship, they disappear
- When large red diamonds are near small blue diamonds, the small diamonds disappear and you gain 1 point
- When large red diamonds transform into sparkling items, your resource count increases
- When enemies hit walls, they reverse direction and continue moving
- Bottom enemies occasionally fire upward projectiles with a small random chance
- Top enemies occasionally fire downward projectiles with a small random chance
- When sparkling items appear and you have collected enough resources, small blue diamonds may spawn elsewhere

Win condition:
You win when all small blue diamonds and large red diamonds have been collected from the level.

Lose condition:
You lose if your spaceship is destroyed by touching walls, enemies, enemy territory, or projectiles.

Objective:
Navigate your spaceship through a dangerous area filled with moving enemies and their projectiles to collect all diamonds while avoiding hazards that will destroy you.
