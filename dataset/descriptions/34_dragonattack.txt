Player role:
This is a two-player game. Player 1 controls a mage that faces downward and can shoot blue orbs downward. Player 2 controls a necromancer that faces upward and can shoot red orbs upward. Each player can only have one projectile on screen at a time.

Entities:
- A mage (player 1's character) that appears as a robed figure facing downward and shoots blue orbs
- A necromancer (player 2's character) that appears as a dark robed figure facing upward and shoots red orbs  
- Blue orbs that move downward at moderate speed, shrink over time, and disappear after a while
- Red orbs that move upward at moderate speed, shrink over time, and disappear after a while
- Two dragons that fly horizontally - one moves left and occasionally shoots blue fire downward, the other moves right and occasionally shoots red fire upward
- Dragon fire projectiles that move slowly and shrink over time - blue fire moves downward, red fire moves upward
- Special dragon kill projectiles that move faster than regular fire - green ones move downward, pink ones move upward
- Colored blocks arranged in layers: green blocks (top layer), blue blocks (middle layer), and red blocks (bottom layer)
- Base structures - one for each player that can be destroyed
- Gold items that appear when bases are hit - yellow mushrooms that give different point values
- Trigger switches that can be activated to change dragon behavior - they appear as treasure chests that open and close
- Walls that block movement
- A hidden background

Interactions:
- When players hit walls, they are pushed back and cannot pass through
- When dragons hit walls, they turn around and are pushed back
- When any projectile hits a wall, the projectile is destroyed
- When blue and red orbs collide, both are destroyed
- When dragon fire projectiles of opposite colors collide, both are destroyed  
- When dragon kill projectiles of opposite colors collide, both are destroyed
- When any player orb hits any dragon fire, both are destroyed
- When a green block is hit by any orb, it transforms into a blue block
- When a green block is hit by a blue orb, both are destroyed and player 1 gets 2 points
- When a green block is hit by a red orb, both are destroyed and player 2 gets 2 points
- When a blue block is hit by any orb, it transforms into a red block
- When a blue block is hit by a blue orb, both are destroyed and player 1 gets 1 point
- When a blue block is hit by a red orb, both are destroyed and player 2 gets 1 point
- When a red block is hit by a blue orb, both are destroyed, player 1 gets 1 point, and player 2 loses 1 point
- When a red block is hit by a red orb, both are destroyed, player 2 gets 1 point, and player 1 loses 1 point
- When a dragon is hit by a blue orb, both are destroyed, player 1 gets 20 points, and player 2 loses 5 points
- When a dragon is hit by a red orb, both are destroyed, player 2 gets 20 points, and player 1 loses 5 points
- When a base is hit by blue dragon fire, the base is destroyed, the fire is destroyed, and a yellow gold item appears
- When a base is hit by red dragon fire, the base is destroyed, the fire is destroyed, and a different yellow gold item appears
- When player 1 touches a yellow gold item, the item is destroyed, player 1 gets 10 points, and player 2 loses 5 points
- When player 2 touches a yellow gold item, the item is destroyed, player 2 gets 10 points, and player 1 loses 5 points
- When player 2 is hit by a green dragon kill projectile, both are destroyed, player 1 gets 1000 points, and player 2 loses 10 points
- When player 1 is hit by a pink dragon kill projectile, both are destroyed, player 2 gets 1000 points, and player 1 loses 10 points
- When any block is hit by a green dragon kill projectile, both are destroyed and player
