Player role:
The player controls an avatar that can aim and shoot. The avatar cannot move through walls or past the edge of the screen - when it tries to do so, it is pushed back to its previous position.

Entities:
- Avatar: The player-controlled character that can aim and fire projectiles
- Blue pad: A stationary blue object that remains in place throughout the game
- Bullet: A projectile that the avatar fires, which is affected by gravity and moves at high speed. Only one bullet can exist at a time
- Wall: Barriers that block movement and destroy bullets on contact

Interactions:
- When a bullet hits a wall, the bullet is destroyed
- When a wall is hit by a bullet, the wall is destroyed  
- When a bullet hits a blue pad, the bullet is destroyed
- When the avatar tries to move into a wall, the avatar is pushed back
- When the avatar tries to move past the edge of the screen, the avatar is pushed back
- When a bullet reaches the edge of the screen, the bullet is destroyed

Win condition:
The player wins when all blue pads have been destroyed.

Lose condition:
The player loses when the avatar is destroyed.

Objective:
Use your avatar to aim and shoot bullets at all the blue pads to destroy them while avoiding getting your avatar destroyed.
