Player role:
The player controls a character that can move in any direction and rotate to face different orientations. The character moves at normal walking speed and can collect ingredients in a specific sequence.

Entities:
- A player character that moves around the kitchen and collects cooking ingredients
- A chef enemy that chases the player character, moving slowly but persistently within a limited range, appearing orange in color
- Flour ingredient that can be collected first in the cooking sequence
- Milk ingredient that can only be collected after obtaining flour
- Egg ingredient that can only be collected after obtaining milk
- Sugar ingredient that can only be collected after obtaining egg
- Butter ingredient that can only be collected after obtaining sugar
- Cherries ingredient that can only be collected after obtaining butter (the final ingredient)
- Tables that serve as immovable obstacles
- Walls that serve as immovable barriers, appearing black
- Floor tiles that provide the walkable surface (invisible to players)

Interactions:
- When the player character touches a wall or table, it is pushed back and cannot pass through
- When the chef touches a wall or table, it is pushed back and cannot pass through  
- When the player character touches the chef, the player character is destroyed and the score decreases by 1 point
- When the player character touches flour, it collects the flour and gains 1 point
- When the player character touches milk while holding flour, it collects the milk and gains 1 point
- When the player character touches egg while holding milk, it collects the egg and gains 1 point
- When the player character touches sugar while holding egg, it collects the sugar and gains 1 point
- When the player character touches butter while holding sugar, it collects the butter and gains 1 point
- When the player character touches cherries while holding butter, it collects the cherries and gains 1 point

Win condition:
The player wins when all cherries have been collected from the level.

Lose condition:
The player loses if they are caught by the chef or if time runs out before collecting all ingredients.

Objective:
Collect all cooking ingredients in the correct sequence (flour, milk, egg, sugar, butter, then cherries) while avoiding the pursuing chef enemy within the time limit.
