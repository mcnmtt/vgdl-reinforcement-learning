**Player role:**
The player controls a bird that can move freely in all directions. The bird starts with 5 health points but can increase its health up to a maximum of 30 points. The bird continuously loses health over time.

**Entities:**
- A bird (the player character) that moves around and has health that decreases automatically
- Grass floor tiles that cover the ground but are invisible during gameplay
- Black trees that act as solid barriers
- A green exit door that serves as the goal
- Small worms scattered around that serve as food
- An invisible food bank that contains multiple food portions

**Interactions:**
- The bird automatically loses 1 health point every 15 time units, and this repeats continuously
- When the bird touches a worm, the bird gains 15 health points and the worm disappears
- When the bird touches the food bank, the bird gains 15 health points, earns 40 points toward the score, and the food bank disappears
- When the bird reaches the exit door, the bird disappears and the player earns 100 points toward the score
- When the bird touches a tree, the bird is pushed back to its previous position

**Win condition:**
The player wins when there are no exit doors remaining (meaning the bird successfully reached the exit).

**Lose condition:**
The player loses when the bird's health reaches zero and disappears from the game.

**Objective:**
Guide the bird to the exit door while collecting food to maintain its health, as the bird continuously loses health over time and will die if it runs out.
