**Player role:**
The player controls a spaceship that can move left and right along the bottom of the screen and fire blue missiles upward. The spaceship cannot move past the edges of the screen.

**Entities:**
- A white base structure that serves as a protective barrier
- A player-controlled spaceship that fires blue missiles upward
- Blue missiles fired by the player's spaceship that travel upward
- Red bombs that fall downward at moderate speed
- Green aliens that move horizontally across the screen, occasionally drop red bombs, and turn around when they reach screen edges
- Blue aliens that move horizontally across the screen, occasionally drop red bombs, and turn around when they reach screen edges
- Invisible spawn points that periodically create blue aliens (every 16 time units, up to 20 total)
- Invisible spawn points that periodically create green aliens (every 12 time units, up to 20 total)

**Interactions:**
- When the spaceship reaches the edge of the screen, it stops and cannot move further
- When aliens reach the edge of the screen, they turn around and move in the opposite direction
- When missiles reach the edge of the screen, they disappear
- When the base is hit by a bomb, both the base and bomb are destroyed
- When the base is hit by the player's missile, both the base and missile are destroyed
- When an alien touches the base, the alien is destroyed
- When an alien touches the spaceship, the spaceship is destroyed and the player loses 1 point
- When a bomb hits the spaceship, the spaceship is destroyed and the player loses 1 point
- When the player's missile hits an alien, the alien is destroyed and the player gains 2 points

**Win condition:**
The player wins when all spawn points have finished creating aliens and no aliens remain on the screen.

**Lose condition:**
The player loses when their spaceship is destroyed by contact with an alien or bomb.

**Objective:**
Defend your spaceship by shooting all the aliens while avoiding their bombs and preventing them from reaching your base.
