Player role:
This is a two-player game. Player A controls an angel-like character that can move in all directions. Player B controls a second angel-like character that also moves in all directions. Both characters cannot pass through walls and will be stopped when they try to move into one.

Entities:
- Floor: Green grass tiles that cover the background and can be walked on
- Cocoons: Blue stationary objects that serve as breeding spots for butterflies
- Angel characters: Two player-controlled sprites that look like angels and can move freely around the level
- Butterflies: Creatures that move randomly around the level at moderate speed with animated wings
- Walls: Tree-like barriers that block movement for all moving characters

Interactions:
- When any moving character (angel or butterfly) tries to move into a wall, they are pushed back and cannot pass through
- When a butterfly touches Player A's angel, the butterfly is destroyed and Player A gains 2 points
- When a butterfly touches Player B's angel, the butterfly is destroyed and Player B gains 2 points  
- When a butterfly touches a cocoon, a new butterfly is created at that location
- When a cocoon touches a butterfly, the butterfly is destroyed

Win condition:
The game continues indefinitely as butterflies can reproduce by touching cocoons.

Lose condition:
The game ends when all cocoons are destroyed, preventing new butterflies from being created.

Objective:
Compete against the other player to catch as many butterflies as possible to earn points, while managing the butterfly population that can reproduce at cocoon locations.
