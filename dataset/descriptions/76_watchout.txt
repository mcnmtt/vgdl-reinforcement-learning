Player role:
This is a two-player game where each player controls their own character that can move around the screen using keyboard controls.

Entities:
- Two player characters: one appears as a girl and the other as a man, both can move in all directions when controlled by their respective players
- Blue stationary blocks that start on the field and can be pushed around by players
- Red stationary blocks that start on the field and can be pushed around by players
- Blue flying blocks that move automatically to the right at moderate speed
- Red flying blocks that move automatically to the left at moderate speed
- Blue boundary edges that form barriers on parts of the screen perimeter
- Red boundary edges that form barriers on other parts of the screen perimeter
- Solid walls that block movement completely

Interactions:
- When either player touches a wall, they are pushed back and cannot pass through
- When the girl touches a red boundary edge, she is pushed back and cannot pass through
- When the man touches a blue boundary edge, he is pushed back and cannot pass through
- When a blue flying block reaches a red boundary edge, it transforms into a red stationary block
- When a red flying block reaches a blue boundary edge, it transforms into a blue stationary block
- When a player touches any block, the block is pushed away from the player
- When a blue stationary block touches a blue boundary edge, it bounces back precisely
- When a red stationary block touches a red boundary edge, it bounces back precisely
- When two blue stationary blocks collide, one disappears and the other becomes a blue flying block
- When two red stationary blocks collide, one disappears and the other becomes a red flying block
- When a blue flying block hits a red stationary block, the red block is destroyed
- When a red flying block hits a blue stationary block, the blue block is destroyed
- When a blue flying block hits the man, it becomes a red flying block
- When a red flying block hits the girl, it becomes a blue flying block

Win condition:
Player 1 (controlling the girl) wins if all red blocks are eliminated from the field. Player 2 (controlling the man) wins if all blue blocks are eliminated from the field.

Lose condition:
A player loses when their opponent eliminates all blocks of their color, or if the total number of blocks drops to 2 or fewer.

Objective:
Each player must strategically move blocks and manipulate flying blocks to eliminate all of their opponent's colored blocks while protecting their own.
