Player role:
The player controls a spaceship that can move horizontally and fire blue missiles upward. The spaceship cannot move off the edge of the screen - it stops when it reaches the boundary.

Entities:
- A white planetary base that remains stationary and serves as the object to protect
- Blue missiles that move upward when fired by the player's spaceship, with only one able to exist at a time
- Red bombs that move downward at moderate speed
- Green aliens that move horizontally at moderate speed, occasionally drop red bombs with a small chance each moment, and must wait a short time between drops
- Blue aliens that behave identically to green aliens - moving horizontally, occasionally dropping red bombs, and having the same cooldown between drops
- Invisible spawn points that periodically create blue aliens at a slower rate, producing up to 20 total aliens
- Invisible spawn points that periodically create green aliens at a faster rate, also producing up to 20 total aliens

Interactions:
- When aliens reach the edge of the screen, they reverse direction and continue moving
- When missiles reach the edge of the screen, they disappear
- When the base is hit by a red bomb, both the base and bomb are destroyed
- When the base is hit by a blue missile, both are destroyed and the player gains 1 point
- When an alien touches the base, the alien is destroyed
- When an alien touches the player's spaceship, the alien is destroyed and the player loses 1 point
- When a red bomb hits the player's spaceship, the bomb is destroyed and the player loses 1 point
- When a blue missile hits an alien, the alien is destroyed and the player gains 2 points

Win condition:
The player wins when all spawn points have finished creating aliens and no aliens remain on the screen.

Lose condition:
The player loses when their spaceship is destroyed.

Objective:
Defend your base by shooting aliens while avoiding their bombs and direct contact, surviving until all enemy waves are eliminated.
