Player role:
You control a yellow spaceship that moves at moderate speed. The spaceship cannot leave the screen - if you try to move it beyond the edge, it will be pushed back to stay within the boundaries.

Entities:
- Yellow spaceship: The player-controlled vessel that moves around the screen
- Downward-moving projectiles: Small fire-like sprites that travel downward at moderate speed and wrap around to the top when they reach the bottom edge
- Upward-moving projectiles: Small fire-like sprites that travel upward at moderate speed and wrap around to the bottom when they reach the top edge
- Top enemies: Necromancer-like sprites that face right and can fire projectiles, with a brief pause between shots
- Bottom enemies: Necromancer-like sprites that face left and can fire projectiles, with a brief pause between shots
- Regular collectible items: Diamond-shaped objects that can be picked up for points
- Special collectible items: Larger diamond-shaped objects that can be collected
- Flickering power-up effects: Sparkle animations that appear briefly
- Walls: Solid barriers that block movement, with connected wall segments forming continuous barriers
- Floor tiles: Gray background surfaces that can be walked on
- Water areas: Blue liquid surfaces that serve as enemy territory

Interactions:
- When the spaceship touches a downward-moving projectile, the projectile is destroyed
- When the spaceship touches an upward-moving projectile, the projectile is destroyed
- When a top enemy touches the spaceship, the spaceship is destroyed and the score increases by 1
- When a bottom enemy touches the spaceship, the spaceship is destroyed and the score increases by 1
- When the spaceship touches a regular collectible item, the item is destroyed and the score increases by 1
- When projectiles reach the edge of the screen, they wrap around to the opposite side
- When the spaceship tries to move beyond the edge of the screen, it is prevented from leaving

Win condition:
You win if you survive for the full time limit of the level.

Lose condition:
You lose if your spaceship is destroyed by touching any enemy.

Objective:
Navigate your spaceship around the level while avoiding enemies and collecting diamond items for points. Survive until the time runs out to complete the level.
