Player role:
The player controls a cyclops character that can move around the screen. When the avatar reaches the edge of the screen, it bounces back and cannot leave the play area.

Entities:
- Light gray walls that form the boundaries and obstacles of the level
- White tight entrance holes that occasionally spawn slow green moles (very rarely, about 0.2% chance)
- Gray wide entrance holes that spawn quick blue moles more frequently (about 1% chance)
- A red cat that moves around randomly and consumes other sprites, shrinking to half size when it does
- Green slow moles that flicker in and out of visibility, appearing for longer periods (up to 60 time units) before disappearing
- Blue quick moles that also flicker in and out of visibility but appear for shorter periods (up to 20 time units) before disappearing
- The player's cyclops avatar that can move freely around the screen

Interactions:
- When any moving object (avatar or cat) reaches the edge of the screen, it bounces back
- When a mole touches the avatar or cat, the mole is destroyed and the player gains 1 point
- When the avatar touches the cat, the avatar is destroyed and the player loses 5 points

Win condition:
The player wins if they survive for 500 time units without being destroyed.

Lose condition:
The player loses if their avatar is destroyed by touching the cat.

Objective:
Survive for as long as possible while collecting moles for points, but avoid the dangerous cat that will destroy you and cost you points.
