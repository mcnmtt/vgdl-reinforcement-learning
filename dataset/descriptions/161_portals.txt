**Player role:**
The player controls a mage character that can move in all four directions using keyboard input. The avatar moves freely around the game area without being affected by gravity or momentum.

**Entities:**
- Floor tiles that form the ground surface throughout the level
- Wall blocks that form barriers and obstacles, with edges that automatically connect to adjacent walls
- A green door that serves as the goal object
- Portal entry points that transport the player to other locations:
  - Light blue entry portals that connect to brown exit portals of the first type
  - Blue entry portals that connect to brown exit portals of the second type
- Brown exit portals where the player emerges after using entry portals
- Red bullets that behave in different ways:
  - Stationary bullets that remain in place
  - Moving bullets that wander randomly at slow speed, changing direction frequently
  - Fast-moving bullets that travel in straight lines:
    - Some move upward continuously
    - Others move leftward continuously

**Interactions:**
- When randomly moving bullets hit walls or structures, they bounce back and change direction
- When the player touches a wall, they are blocked and cannot pass through
- When the player reaches the green goal door, the door disappears and the player earns one point
- When the player touches any bullet, the player is destroyed
- When straight-moving bullets hit walls, they reverse direction and continue moving
- When the player enters a portal entry point, they are instantly transported to the corresponding exit portal

**Win condition:**
The player wins when all goal doors have been collected (when there are no more green doors remaining on the level).

**Lose condition:**
The player loses if their character is destroyed by touching a bullet.

**Objective:**
Navigate through the level using portals to avoid bullets and collect all the green goal doors without getting hit by any of the moving or stationary bullets.
