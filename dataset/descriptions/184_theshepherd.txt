Player role:
The player controls a character that can move in four directions and shoot dog treats. The player can only carry one treat at a time and must collect more treats from the game world to replenish their supply.

Entities:
- Floor: Hidden grass tiles that cover the ground throughout the level
- Scared sheep: Blue sheep that flee from the player, moving at normal speed
- Seeking sheep: Sheep that move toward entry points, also moving at normal speed
- Tamed sheep: Sheep that move randomly and slowly after being successfully herded
- Hidden auxiliary markers: Invisible objects used internally for game logic
- Player character: The controllable character that can shoot dog treats
- Hungry dog: A dog that chases after dog treats when they appear
- Helpful dog: A dog that chases after scared sheep to help herd them
- Dog treat: An item the player shoots that can be picked up by dogs, bounces off the player, and disappears when hitting walls or fences
- Treat supply: Brown collectible items that replenish the player's ability to shoot dog treats
- Entry points: Invisible locations where sheep can be converted to tamed sheep
- Door: An opening that blocks tamed sheep from passing through
- Fence: Barrier walls made of spikes that block movement
- Wall: Barrier walls made of trees that block movement

Interactions:
- Sheep bounce back when touching walls or fences
- Sheep bounce back when touching other untamed sheep
- The player bounces back when touching walls or fences
- Dogs bounce back when touching walls or fences
- Tamed sheep bounce forward when touching the player
- Dog treats bounce forward when touching the player
- Dog treats disappear when touching walls or fences
- When a hungry dog reaches a dog treat, the dog becomes helpful and the treat disappears
- When a helpful dog reaches a scared sheep, the sheep transforms into an auxiliary marker
- When a helpful dog touches an auxiliary marker, the dog becomes hungry again
- When a hungry dog touches an auxiliary marker, the marker becomes a seeking sheep
- Tamed sheep bounce back when touching doors
- All sheep bounce back when touching fences
- When scared sheep reach entry points, they become tamed sheep and the player scores 5 points
- When seeking sheep reach entry points, they become tamed sheep and the player scores 1 point
- The player can collect treat supplies to replenish ammunition

Win condition:
The player wins when all untamed sheep (both scared and seeking sheep) have been converted to tamed sheep.

Lose condition:
There is no explicit lose condition in this game.

Objective:
Guide all the sheep into the pen by using dog treats to control the dog's behavior, making it help herd the scared sheep toward entry points where they become tamed.
