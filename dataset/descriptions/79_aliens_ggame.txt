**Player role:**
The player controls a spaceship that can move left and right across the bottom of the screen. The spaceship can fire blue missiles upward, but can only have one missile on screen at a time.

**Entities:**
- A spaceship (the player's character) that moves horizontally at the bottom of the screen and fires blue missiles upward
- Blue alien ships that move horizontally at moderate speed and occasionally drop red bombs downward
- Green alien ships that move horizontally at moderate speed and occasionally drop red bombs downward  
- Two types of invisible spawn points: one that creates green aliens every 12 time units (up to 20 total), and another that creates blue aliens every 16 time units (up to 20 total)
- Red bombs that fall downward slowly after being dropped by aliens
- Blue missiles that travel upward when fired by the player's spaceship
- Gray walls that block movement
- A white base or planet that sits stationary
- A space background that fills empty areas

**Interactions:**
- When aliens or the player's spaceship hit the base, they reverse direction and the player loses 2 points
- When aliens hit the player's spaceship, the spaceship takes damage and the player loses 2 points
- When spawn points reach the edge of the screen, they bounce back toward the center
- When aliens reach the edge of the screen, they turn around and continue moving in the opposite direction
- All movements that would go past the screen edge are prevented

**Win condition:**
The player wins by surviving for 1023 time units without being destroyed.

**Lose condition:**
The player loses if their spaceship is destroyed.

**Objective:**
Survive waves of alien attackers for as long as possible while protecting your base, using your limited firepower to defend against incoming enemies and their bombs.
