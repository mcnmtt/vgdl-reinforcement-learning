**Player role:**
The player controls an avatar that can move in all directions and shoot a sword weapon. The avatar can fire the sword at a moderate rate to break through dirt blocks.

**Entities:**
- Avatar: A spelunker character that the player controls, can move freely and shoot a sword
- Sword: A gray pickaxe-like weapon that flickers briefly when fired, only one can exist at a time
- Wall: Brown dirt walls that block movement and have connected tile patterns
- Dirt: Brown dirt blocks that can be destroyed
- Diamond: Yellow gem resources that shrink slightly when collected, up to 10 can exist
- Boulder: Gray rock that falls downward slowly due to gravity
- Exit door: A green door that serves as the level exit
- Crab: A red scorpion-like enemy that chases the avatar slowly
- Butterfly: A pink bat-like enemy that chases the avatar slowly

**Interactions:**
- When the sword hits dirt, the dirt is destroyed
- When the avatar touches a diamond, the diamond is collected and the player gains 2 points
- When any moving object (avatar or enemies) hits a wall or boulder, they are pushed back and cannot pass through
- When the avatar is hit by a boulder from above, the avatar is destroyed and the player loses 1 point
- When the avatar touches a butterfly or crab, the avatar is destroyed and the player loses 1 point
- When an enemy is hit by a boulder, the enemy is destroyed and the player gains 5 points
- When a boulder hits dirt, walls, diamonds, or other boulders, it stops moving
- When dirt is touched by a butterfly, the butterfly is destroyed
- When dirt is touched by a crab, the crab transforms into a diamond
- When a crab and butterfly touch each other, both are destroyed
- When a butterfly touches a crab, the butterfly transforms into a diamond and the player gains 1 point
- When the avatar reaches the exit door while carrying at least 10 diamonds, the avatar exits the level

**Win condition:**
The player wins when the avatar successfully exits through the door (which requires collecting at least 10 diamonds first).

**Lose condition:**
The player loses when the avatar is destroyed by touching an enemy or being crushed by a falling boulder.

**Objective:**
Navigate through the underground cavern, collect at least 10 diamonds while avoiding or eliminating enemies and falling boulders, then reach the exit door to escape.
