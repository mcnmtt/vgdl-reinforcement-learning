Player role:
The player controls an avatar that moves with inertia, meaning it continues moving in the direction it was pushed and gradually comes to a stop rather than stopping immediately when input ends.

Entities:
- Avatar: The player-controlled character that moves with inertia physics
- Blue pads: Stationary blue objects that remain fixed in place
- Walls: Immovable barriers that form the boundaries of the level

Interactions:
- When the avatar or any object with inertia hits a wall, it bounces off in the opposite direction
- When the avatar touches a blue pad, the pad is destroyed

Win condition:
The player wins when all blue pads have been destroyed.

Lose condition:
The player loses if the avatar is destroyed.

Objective:
Navigate your avatar around the level to collect all the blue pads while avoiding destruction, using the inertial movement physics to bounce off walls and reach your targets.
