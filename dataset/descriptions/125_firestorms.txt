**Player role:**
The player controls an avatar that can move in four directions around the game area. The avatar starts with 1 health point.

**Entities:**
- Floor: An invisible gray surface that forms the background of the game area
- Fire projectiles: Orange moving objects that spawn randomly and move in random directions at moderate speed
- Spawn points: Red objects that randomly create fire projectiles with a 25% chance, appearing smaller than normal fire
- Water: Blue collectible resources with a maximum of 10 that can appear in the game, each worth 2 points
- Escape door: A green open door that serves as the exit point
- Avatar: The player character, depicted as a druid
- Walls: Dark gray immovable barriers that block movement

**Interactions:**
- When the avatar touches a wall, it is pushed back and cannot pass through
- When the avatar reaches the escape door, the door disappears
- When the avatar touches water, it gains health points and the water is consumed
- When the avatar touches fire, it loses health points and loses 1 point from the score
- When fire touches water, an avatar, or a wall, the fire is destroyed

**Win condition:**
The player wins when there are no escape doors remaining on the screen (meaning the avatar has reached the exit).

**Lose condition:**
The player loses when the avatar is destroyed (health reaches zero).

**Objective:**
Navigate through a dangerous area filled with randomly spawning fire projectiles, collect water to maintain your health, and reach the green escape door to win while avoiding being destroyed by the fire.
