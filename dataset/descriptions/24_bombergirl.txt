**Player role:**
This is a two-player game. Player 1 controls a rogue character that can move in four directions and shoot blue bombs. Player 2 controls a spelunky character that can move in four directions and shoot red bombs. Both players move on a grid-based system.

**Entities:**
- Ground: invisible grass tiles that form the walkable surface
- Player 1's avatar: a rogue character that can shoot blue bombs
- Player 2's avatar: a spelunky character that can shoot red bombs  
- Blue minion: a small blue ogre that randomly drops blue bombs with a 5% chance, waiting at least 10 steps between drops
- Red minion: a small red ogre that randomly drops red bombs with a 5% chance, waiting at least 10 steps between drops
- Boxes: immovable block obstacles that can be destroyed
- Blue bombs: explosive devices that detonate after being placed, only one can exist at a time per player
- Red bombs: explosive devices that detonate after being placed, only one can exist at a time per player
- Blue explosion center: the initial explosion point that spreads outward with 100% probability
- Red explosion center: the initial explosion point that spreads outward with 100% probability
- Blue sparks: explosion effects that spread from the explosion center and create final explosion effects
- Red sparks: explosion effects that spread from the explosion center and create final explosion effects
- Blue final explosions: the lasting explosion effects that disappear after a short time
- Red final explosions: the lasting explosion effects that disappear after a short time
- Walls: immovable barriers that block movement and stop explosions

**Interactions:**
- When any movable object touches a wall, box, or bomb, it is pushed back and cannot pass through
- When a bomb touches a wall, box, or avatar, the bomb is destroyed
- When a final explosion touches a wall, the explosion is destroyed
- When a box is touched by blue explosions or blue final explosions, the box is destroyed and Player 1 scores 1 point
- When a box is touched by red explosions or red final explosions, the box is destroyed and Player 2 scores 1 point
- When Player 2's avatar is touched by blue explosions or blue final explosions, Player 2's avatar is destroyed and Player 1 scores 50 points
- When a red minion is touched by blue explosions or blue final explosions, the red minion is destroyed and Player 1 scores 10 points
- When Player 1's avatar is touched by red explosions or red final explosions, Player 1's avatar is destroyed and Player 2 scores 50 points
- When a blue minion is touched by red explosions or red final explosions, the blue minion is destroyed and Player 2 scores 10 points

**Win condition:**
Player 1 wins if Player 2's avatar is destroyed. Player 2 wins if Player 1's avatar is destroyed. If all boxes are destroyed or time runs out, the player with the higher score wins.

**Lose condition:**
A player loses if their avatar is destroyed by the opponent's explosions.

**Objective:**
Destroy your opponent's avatar with your bombs while avoiding their explosions, or score more points by destroying boxes and enemy minions before time runs out.
