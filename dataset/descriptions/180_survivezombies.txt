**Player role:**
The player controls a sword-wielding character that moves in response to player input. The character has a health system, starting with 1 health point but capable of gaining up to 10 health points maximum.

**Entities:**
- Floor tiles that serve as the basic ground surface
- Pink flowers that occasionally spawn yellow bees (2% chance each moment)
- Red tombstones that spawn brown zombies - some spawn zombies frequently (5% chance each moment) while others spawn them less often (3% chance each moment)
- Golden honey drops that remain stationary once placed
- The player's sword-wielding character that can move around and has a health system
- Yellow bees that move randomly around the game area at normal speed, with brief pauses between movements
- Brown zombies that actively chase the player character at half speed, with longer pauses between movements
- Walls that block movement and cannot be destroyed

**Interactions:**
- When the player character touches honey, the character gains one health point, the honey disappears, and the player gains one point
- When any moving character (player, bee, or zombie) hits a wall, they are pushed back and cannot pass through
- When the player character touches a zombie, the player loses one health point and loses one point
- When a zombie touches the player character, the player character is destroyed
- When a bee touches a zombie, the zombie is destroyed and transforms into a honey drop
- When the player character touches a tombstone, the character is destroyed and the player loses one point

**Win condition:**
The player wins if they survive for 1000 time units without being destroyed.

**Lose condition:**
The player loses if their character is destroyed by touching a zombie or tombstone.

**Objective:**
Survive in an arena filled with zombie-spawning tombstones by avoiding enemies and collecting honey (created when bees destroy zombies) to maintain your health, while lasting long enough to achieve victory through endurance.
