Player role:
The player controls a character that can move in four directions using keyboard input. The character can transform into different forms by collecting special items, which changes both its appearance and abilities.

Entities:
- A floor that covers the ground throughout the level, providing the basic walking surface
- Spike traps that are dangerous obstacles placed on the floor
- A green exit door that serves as the level's endpoint
- Gray normal walls that block movement for all character forms
- Red dirt walls that can be passed through only by the red-cloaked character form
- Blue stone walls that can be passed through only by the blue-cloaked character form
- A red cloak that transforms the character into a red-cloaked form when collected
- A blue cloak that transforms the character into a blue-cloaked form when collected
- The player character, which starts in normal form as a girl but can transform into a red princess form or blue queen form by collecting the corresponding cloaks

Interactions:
- When any character form touches a normal wall, it is pushed back and cannot pass through
- When the normal character form touches any colored wall, it is pushed back and cannot pass through
- When the blue character form touches a red wall, it is pushed back and cannot pass through
- When the red character form touches a blue wall, it is pushed back and cannot pass through
- When the character touches a red cloak, it transforms into the red form, the cloak disappears, and the player gains 3 points
- When the character touches a blue cloak, it transforms into the blue form, the cloak disappears, and the player gains 3 points
- When the character reaches the exit door, the character disappears and the player gains 1 point
- When the character touches a spike trap, the character is destroyed and the player loses 1 point

Win condition:
The player wins when the character successfully reaches and enters the exit door, causing it to disappear from the level.

Lose condition:
The player loses if the character is destroyed by touching a spike trap, or if 1000 time units pass without completing the level.

Objective:
Navigate through a maze-like level by collecting colored cloaks to transform your character and gain the ability to pass through matching colored walls, ultimately reaching the exit door while avoiding deadly spike traps.
