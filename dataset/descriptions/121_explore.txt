**Player role:**
The player controls a character that can move in four directions and shoot projectiles. The character bounces back when hitting walls or the edge of the screen.

**Entities:**
- Floor tiles that are invisible but mark walkable areas
- Yellow button blocks that can be activated
- Blue button blocks that appear when yellow buttons are activated
- Hidden exit zones that become visible doors when activated
- Flag doors that appear when buttons are properly activated
- Guards that patrol the area, chase the player when they have line of sight, move at half normal speed, and appear smaller than normal size
- Black walls that block movement and cause sprites to bounce back
- The player character (a girl in red) that can shoot projectiles
- Flickering projectiles that the player shoots, which disappear after a short time

**Interactions:**
- When guards hit other guards, walls, or the edge of the screen, they bounce back
- When the player hits walls, they bounce back  
- When the player touches a guard, the player is destroyed
- When a projectile hits a yellow button, all hidden exits transform into visible flag doors
- When a projectile hits a yellow button, that button transforms into a blue button
- When the player touches a flag door, a winner object is created

**Win condition:**
The player wins when a winner object is created (by reaching an activated exit door).

**Lose condition:**
The player loses if they are destroyed by touching a guard, or if 1000 time units pass.

**Objective:**
Navigate through the level while avoiding guards, shoot the yellow buttons to activate the exit doors, then reach an exit door to win.
