**Player role:**
The player controls a firefighter that can move in all directions and shoot water. The firefighter has 10 health points and starts with a limited supply of water ammunition that can be replenished.

**Entities:**
- Ground: Hidden street tiles that form the walkable surface
- Water ammunition: Blue resource items that track the player's water supply, with a maximum limit of 10
- Water projectiles: Blue slime-like sprites that the firefighter shoots, moving at normal speed and shrinking slightly over time
- Boxes: Stationary city building sprites that block movement
- Fire extinguishers: Stationary hydrant sprites that provide water refills
- Fire locations: Areas where fire can spawn, appearing as burning city buildings
- Active fire spreaders: Orange fire sprites that randomly spread to adjacent areas with a 60% probability
- Burned areas: Stationary sprites representing areas where fire has finished spreading
- Firefighter avatar: The player character appearing as a fireman sprite
- Walls: Stationary wall sprites that block movement

**Interactions:**
- When the firefighter touches walls or boxes, it is pushed back and cannot pass through
- When boxes touch burned areas, the box is destroyed and the player loses 1 point
- When burned areas touch boxes, the burned area transforms back into an active fire location
- When fire touches walls, the fire is destroyed
- When fire and water projectiles touch each other, both are destroyed and the player gains 2 points
- When water projectiles hit fire, boxes, or walls, the water projectile is destroyed
- When the firefighter touches fire, the firefighter loses health points
- When burned areas touch the ground, they are destroyed
- When the firefighter touches fire extinguishers, the water ammunition supply increases by 2

**Win condition:**
The player wins when all fire sprites are eliminated from the level.

**Lose condition:**
The player loses if the firefighter's health reaches zero or if the time limit of 1500 time units expires.

**Objective:**
Extinguish all fires by shooting water at them while avoiding direct contact with flames and preventing the fire from spreading to and destroying buildings.
