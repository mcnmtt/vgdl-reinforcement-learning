**Player role:**
You control a police officer who moves in four directions using keyboard input. The officer moves at normal speed and is not affected by gravity or special physics.

**Entities:**
- Floor tiles that provide the basic walkable surface, invisible to the player
- Walls that block movement for all characters
- A jail door (white) that can only be opened with a key
- A depot (white, key-shaped icon) where you can pick up keys when you don't have one
- A jail building (white) where you deposit captured criminals
- A key (light blue) that you can carry to open jail doors - you can only hold one at a time
- Red thugs that flee from you at high speed with animated sprites
- Green thugs that flee from you at high speed with animated sprites  
- Yellow thugs that flee from you at high speed with animated sprites
- A boss thug that flees from you at high speed with animated sprites
- Invisible transaction zones that appear at the jail when you have criminals to deposit
- Resource counters that track how many of each color criminal you've captured

**Interactions:**
- You bounce back when walking into walls
- All thugs bounce back when walking into walls or jail doors
- When you touch a key, you pick it up and the key disappears
- When you touch a jail door while carrying a key, the door opens, you lose the key, but are pushed back
- When you touch the depot without a key, a new key appears there
- When you touch any color thug, you capture them (they disappear and increase your count of that color criminal)
- When you touch the jail while carrying green criminals, an invisible green transaction zone appears
- When you touch a green transaction zone, you deposit one green criminal and gain 2 points
- When you touch the jail while carrying yellow criminals, an invisible yellow transaction zone appears  
- When you touch a yellow transaction zone, you deposit one yellow criminal and gain 5 points
- When you touch the jail while carrying red criminals, an invisible red transaction zone appears
- When you touch a red transaction zone, you deposit one red criminal and gain 100 points
- When the boss thug touches you, you die but gain 100 points

**Win condition:**
You win when you defeat the boss thug (there are no more boss thugs remaining on the level).

**Lose condition:**
You lose if the time limit of 1900 game ticks expires, or if the boss thug catches and kills you.

**Objective:**
Capture criminals by chasing them down, then deposit them at the jail for points while avoiding the dangerous boss thug. Defeat the boss to win the level.
