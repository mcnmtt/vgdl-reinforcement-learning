Player role:
The player controls a spaceship that moves in any direction at moderate speed and has a health system starting with 15 health points out of a maximum of 20. The spaceship is destroyed if it reaches the edge of the screen.

Entities:
- A spaceship (the player's avatar) that can move freely and has health points
- Nebulae that appear as explosion-like clouds and damage anything that touches them
- Black holes that appear as whirlpools and destroy anything that enters them
- Turrets that look like satellites, rotate in place, and randomly fire bombs with a 40% chance every 3 seconds
- Red circular bombs that move quickly in straight lines and gradually shrink as they travel
- Empty planets that appear as one type of planet image
- Busy planets that appear as a different type of planet image
- Invisible atmosphere zones around planets that cannot be seen
- Background space tiles that fill empty areas

Interactions:
- When the spaceship touches a nebula, it loses health points and the score decreases by 1
- When the spaceship reaches the edge of the screen, it is destroyed
- When the spaceship encounters a bomb, the bomb turns toward the spaceship
- When the spaceship touches a bomb, it loses health points
- When a bomb hits the spaceship, the bomb is destroyed
- When the spaceship touches an empty planet, it aligns with the planet's position
- When the spaceship touches an empty planet, that planet transforms into a busy planet
- When the spaceship touches any planet, it gains health points
- When the spaceship enters a planet's atmosphere, a 2-second timer starts that will transform all busy planets back into empty planets
- When the spaceship enters a black hole, it is destroyed but the score increases by 1

Win condition:
The player wins when all black holes have been removed from the level.

Lose condition:
The player loses if the spaceship is destroyed or if 1000 time units pass without completing the objective.

Objective:
Navigate your spaceship through space to eliminate all black holes while avoiding nebulae and turret fire, using planets to restore health when needed.
