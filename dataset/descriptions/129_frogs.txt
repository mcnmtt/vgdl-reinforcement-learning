**Player role:**
The player controls a character that can move in all four directions using arrow keys or similar controls. The character moves one space at a time and stops immediately when not being directed.

**Entities:**
- **Grass**: Green ground tiles that form the basic terrain
- **Highway**: Road surface that serves as pathways for vehicles
- **Dense forest spawner**: Blue water-colored areas that frequently create new logs (40% chance every 10 time units)
- **Sparse forest spawner**: Blue water-colored areas that occasionally create new logs (10% chance every 5 time units)
- **Water**: Blue impassable barriers that block movement
- **Goal**: Green exit door that the player must reach to complete the level
- **Log**: Brown objects that move leftward slowly and can be ridden by the player
- **Fast orange trucks**: Orange vehicles that move quickly (either leftward or rightward depending on type)
- **Slow red trucks**: Red vehicles that move slowly (either leftward or rightward depending on type)
- **Player character**: The main character controlled by the player
- **Walls**: Black impassable tree barriers that block movement

**Interactions:**
- When the player touches the goal, the goal disappears and the player scores a point
- When the player touches a log, the player is protected from being destroyed by water and moves along with the log
- When the player touches a wall or reaches the edge of the screen, the player is pushed back to their previous position
- When the player touches a truck or water (without log protection), the player is destroyed
- When a log reaches the edge of the screen, it disappears
- When a truck reaches the edge of the screen, it wraps around to the opposite side

**Win condition:**
The player wins when all goals have been collected (no goals remain on the screen).

**Lose condition:**
The player loses when their character is destroyed by touching trucks or water without protection.

**Objective:**
Navigate safely across dangerous highways filled with moving trucks and use floating logs to cross water obstacles in order to reach the exit door.
