Player role:
This is a two-player game. Player 1 controls a knight character that can move in four directions using keyboard input. Player 2 controls a guard character that can also move in four directions. Both characters move by stepping from square to square on the game grid.

Entities:
- A knight character controlled by player 1 that moves around the level
- A guard character controlled by player 2 that moves around the level  
- Light-colored boxes belonging to player 1 that can be pushed around
- Light-colored boxes belonging to player 2 that can be pushed around
- Dark-colored boxes belonging to player 1 that appear when light boxes fall into holes
- Dark-colored boxes belonging to player 2 that appear when light boxes fall into holes
- Blue holes scattered around the level that boxes can fall into
- Floor tiles that make up the ground surface
- Wall tiles that block movement and are automatically connected to adjacent walls

Interactions:
- When either character touches a wall, they are prevented from moving into that space
- When either character touches any box, the box is pushed forward in the direction the character was moving
- When any box touches a wall, all boxes return to their previous positions
- When a light box belonging to player 1 touches a hole, it transforms into a dark box and player 1 gains one point
- When a light box belonging to player 2 touches a hole, it transforms into a dark box and player 2 gains one point
- When a dark box belonging to player 1 touches the ground, it transforms back into a light box and player 1 loses one point
- When a dark box belonging to player 2 touches the ground, it transforms back into a light box and player 2 loses one point

Win condition:
Player 1 wins when there are no light boxes belonging to player 1 remaining on the level. Player 2 wins when there are no light boxes belonging to player 2 remaining on the level.

Lose condition:
A player loses when their opponent successfully gets all of their own light boxes into holes first.

Objective:
Each player must push all of their light-colored boxes into the blue holes to transform them into dark boxes while preventing them from touching the ground again. The first player to get all their boxes transformed wins.
