Player role:
The player controls a character that can move in four directions around the game area. The character has two states: one without a key and one with a key, which changes its appearance.

Entities:
- A floor that serves as the walkable surface throughout the game area, invisible to the player
- Dark blue holes scattered around the level that act as pits
- The player's character, which appears as a swordsman that animates while moving and changes appearance when carrying a key
- Mushrooms that remain stationary and can be collected for points
- A key that can be picked up by the player
- A goal door that remains closed until the player has the key
- Movable boxes that can be pushed around the level
- Walls made of dirt that block movement and have connected tile appearance

Interactions:
- When the player touches a wall, they are pushed back and cannot pass through
- When the player falls into a hole, they are destroyed
- When the player pushes against a box, the box slides forward in the direction of the push
- When a box hits a wall, another box, or a mushroom, the box returns to its previous position
- When a box falls into a hole, both the box and hole disappear and the player gains 1 point
- When the player (without a key) touches the key, they transform into the key-carrying version
- When the player (with a key) touches the key, the key disappears
- When the player approaches the goal door without a key, they are pushed back
- When the player with a key touches the goal door, the door disappears and the player gains 5 points
- When the player touches a mushroom, the mushroom disappears and the player gains 1 point

Win condition:
The player wins when all goal doors have been removed from the level.

Lose condition:
The player loses if their character is destroyed by falling into a hole.

Objective:
Push boxes into holes to fill them while collecting the key and reaching the goal door. Use the boxes strategically to create safe paths and collect mushrooms for extra points.
