Player role:
The player controls a character that can move around and shoot arrows. The character moves at moderate speed and can shoot one arrow at a time - a new arrow can only be fired after the previous one hits something or leaves the screen.

Entities:
- A character (the player) that appears as a spelunky-style explorer and can shoot arrows
- Arrows that the player shoots, which fly straight and fast in the direction fired
- A green exit door that serves as the goal
- Black walls that block movement
- Floor sections that provide flat surfaces
- Ground areas that serve as the base terrain
- Ladders that allow vertical movement between levels
- A monkey character that moves around very slowly and randomly
- Invisible spawning points that periodically create barrels - some spawn barrels moving right, others spawn barrels moving left
- Various types of barrels:
  - Falling barrels that drop straight down at moderate speed (some appear as light brown barrels, others as dark brown barrels)
  - Rolling barrels that move horizontally along the ground at slow speed (light brown breakable ones and dark brown tough ones)
- When falling barrels hit the floor, light brown ones become breakable rolling barrels and dark brown ones become tough rolling barrels, maintaining their left or right direction
- When rolling barrels encounter ladders, they transform back into falling barrels and drop down

Interactions:
- When the player touches a wall or reaches the edge of the screen, they are pushed back and cannot pass through
- When the player touches any type of barrel (falling or rolling), the player is destroyed
- When a falling barrel hits the floor, it stops falling and becomes a rolling barrel moving in the same direction
- When a rolling barrel hits a ladder, it becomes a falling barrel and drops straight down
- When a rolling barrel hits a wall, it reverses direction and continues rolling
- When a rolling barrel reaches the edge of the screen, it disappears
- When an arrow hits a wall or reaches the edge of the screen, the arrow disappears
- When an arrow hits a breakable (light brown) barrel, both the arrow and barrel are destroyed and the player scores 1 point
- When the player reaches the goal exit, the goal disappears and the player scores 1 point

Win condition:
The player wins when they reach and enter the goal exit (when no goal sprites remain on screen).

Lose condition:
The player loses if they are destroyed by touching any barrel.

Objective:
Navigate through the level while avoiding dangerous barrels, use arrows to destroy breakable barrels that block your path, and reach the green exit door to win.
