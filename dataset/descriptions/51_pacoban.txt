Player role:
This is a two-player Pac-Man style game. Player A controls a yellow character that can move in four directions and wraps around the screen edges when reaching them. Player B controls an identical yellow character. Both players move at normal speed and cannot rotate in place. When a player collects a power orb, they temporarily transform into an orange powered-up form that can destroy ghosts.

Entities:
- Floor tiles that form the walkable surface of the maze
- Wall blocks that form barriers and automatically connect to adjacent walls
- Small yellow pellets worth 1 point each
- Pink cherries (fruit) worth 5 points each  
- Green power orbs worth 10 points that give temporary ghost-destroying abilities
- Four different colored ghosts (red, blue, pink, orange) that normally chase the players but flee when players are powered up
- Invisible spawn points where ghosts respawn after being destroyed

Interactions:
- When a player hits a wall, they bounce back and stop moving forward
- When walls touch each other, any conflicting movements are undone
- When a wall reaches the edge of the screen, it is destroyed
- When the two players collide, they both step back from each other
- When a ghost hits a wall, it steps back and changes direction
- When a player reaches the edge of the screen, they wrap around to the opposite side
- When a ghost reaches the edge of the screen while another ghost is there, the first ghost steps back
- When an unpowered player touches a ghost, the player is destroyed and loses 1 point
- When a player collects a power orb, all ghosts transform from their normal chasing state to a fleeing state for a limited time, the power orb is destroyed, the player gains 10 points, and the player becomes powered up temporarily
- When a player collects a pellet, the pellet is destroyed and the player gains 1 point
- When a player collects fruit, the fruit is destroyed and the player gains 5 points  
- When a powered-up player touches a ghost, the ghost is destroyed and the player gains 40 points

Win condition:
Player A wins if all of Player B's characters are destroyed, or if more food items are collected when time runs out or all food is eaten. Player B wins if all of Player A's characters are destroyed.

Lose condition:
A player loses if their character is destroyed by touching a ghost while unpowered, or if they collect fewer food items than their opponent when the game ends.

Objective:
Compete against the other player to collect pellets, fruit, and power orbs while avoiding ghosts, and try to eliminate your opponent by having them run into ghosts.
