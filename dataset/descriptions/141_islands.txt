**Player role:**
The player controls an avatar that can move in four directions and shoot shovels. The avatar can carry up to 10 units of sand as a resource. When moving into water, the avatar automatically uses one unit of sand to turn the water into dry land, but dies if it has no sand when touching water.

**Entities:**
- **Avatar**: The player character that moves around, shoots shovels, and collects sand
- **Water**: Blue liquid terrain that blocks movement and kills the avatar if it has no sand
- **Dry land without sand**: Brown solid ground that can be walked on safely
- **Dry land with sand**: Beige solid ground that contains sand deposits
- **Shovel**: A gray projectile that the avatar can shoot, which disappears after one use
- **Bomb**: A stationary explosive object that blocks movement
- **Spark generator**: A sparkling object that creates fire when activated
- **Starting fire**: Orange flames that spread automatically to adjacent spaces
- **Ending fire**: Orange flames that don't spread but destroy most objects they touch
- **Whirlpool**: A swirling water hazard that kills the avatar on contact
- **Goal**: A hole that serves as the level exit
- **Sand**: Yellow granules that can be collected as a resource (up to 10 units)
- **Treasure**: A valuable chest that gives points when collected

**Interactions:**
- The avatar bounces back when hitting the edge of the screen, walls, or bombs
- The avatar dies when touched by ending fire
- When a bomb is hit by ending fire or a shovel, it transforms into a spark generator
- When water is touched by ending fire, it becomes dry land without sand
- Ending fire is destroyed when it touches land, whirlpools, goals, treasure, the edge of the screen, or walls
- When two ending fire sprites meet, they both turn into dry land without sand
- The avatar collects sand when touching it
- The avatar dies if it touches water while having no sand
- When the avatar has sand and touches water, it loses one sand and the water becomes dry land without sand
- The avatar dies when touching treasure but gains 100 points
- When a shovel hits sandy land and the avatar has sand, it spawns sand granules
- When sand touches sandy land, the land becomes regular dry land
- When a shovel hits water and the avatar has more than 1 sand, it spawns sand granules
- The avatar dies when touching a whirlpool
- The avatar dies when touching the goal but gains 1 point (completing the level)

**Win condition:**
The player wins when all goals have been removed from the level (by touching them).

**Lose condition:**
The player loses if the avatar dies or if 1000 time units pass without completing the level.

**Objective:**
Navigate through a water-filled landscape by collecting sand and using it to create safe paths across water, while avoiding fire and whirlpools, to reach the goal and complete the level.
