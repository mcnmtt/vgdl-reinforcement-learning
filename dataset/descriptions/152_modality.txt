**Player role:**
The player controls a girl character who can move in four directions using arrow keys or WASD. The character changes appearance and behavior based on which colored surface she's standing on - she appears differently when on black surfaces, white surfaces, or neutral grey surfaces.

**Entities:**
- Black tiles: Immovable black floor surfaces that form part of the game area
- White tiles: Immovable white floor surfaces that form part of the game area  
- Grey tiles: Immovable brown-colored neutral surfaces
- Targets: Holes that act as destinations for crates, appearing as dark circular pits
- Win targets: Holes that have been successfully filled with crates
- Crates: Movable brown bush-like objects that can be pushed around
- Avatar variations: The player character appears as different colored girls depending on the surface type (dark girl on black, light girl on white, standard girl on grey)
- Walls: Immovable dirt-textured barriers that block movement

**Interactions:**
- When the avatar tries to move beyond the edge of the screen, it is prevented from moving
- When the avatar is on a black surface and tries to move onto a white surface, it is prevented from moving
- When the avatar is on a white surface and tries to move onto a black surface, it is prevented from moving
- When the avatar is on grey surfaces and moves onto black surfaces, it transforms into the black surface version
- When the avatar is on grey surfaces and moves onto white surfaces, it transforms into the white surface version
- When the avatar moves onto grey surfaces, it transforms into the neutral grey version
- When the avatar pushes against a crate, the crate is pushed forward in the same direction
- When a crate hits a wall, all recent moves are undone
- When a target hits a wall, all recent moves are undone
- When the avatar hits a wall, it is prevented from moving
- When a crate is pushed into a target hole, the target transforms into a win target and the score increases by 1

**Win condition:**
The player wins when all target holes have been filled with crates (when there are no unfilled targets remaining).

**Lose condition:**
There is no explicit lose condition - the game continues until won.

**Objective:**
Push all the crates into the target holes while navigating the color-restricted movement system where you can only move between matching colored surfaces or through grey transition areas.
