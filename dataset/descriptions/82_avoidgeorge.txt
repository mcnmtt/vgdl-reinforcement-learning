Player role:
The player controls a character that can move around and shoot cigarettes. The player can rotate in place and fire cigarettes as projectiles.

Entities:
- Floor: Hidden ground tiles that provide the base surface for the level
- Player character: A controllable character that can move around and shoot cigarettes
- Quiet citizens: Non-player characters that move randomly around the level at moderate speed
- Annoyed citizens: Non-player characters that move randomly around the level at moderate speed and appear more agitated
- George: A character that actively chases after quiet citizens at moderate speed
- Cigarette: A flickering projectile that can be shot by the player, with a maximum of 5 existing at once, and only one can be fired at a time
- Wall: Immovable barrier objects that block movement

Interactions:
- When George touches a quiet citizen, the quiet citizen becomes an annoyed citizen
- When George touches the player, the player is destroyed and the score decreases by 1
- When a cigarette touches an annoyed citizen, the annoyed citizen becomes a quiet citizen and the score increases by 1
- When any human character touches a wall, they are pushed back from the wall

Win condition:
The player wins if they survive for the full time limit of 1000 time units.

Lose condition:
The player loses if their character is destroyed by George, or if all quiet citizens are eliminated from the game.

Objective:
Survive George's pursuit while using cigarettes to calm down annoyed citizens and prevent all quiet citizens from being eliminated before the time runs out.
