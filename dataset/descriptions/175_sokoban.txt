**Player role:**
The player controls a knight character that moves in response to key presses. The knight moves on a grid-based floor and can push objects around.

**Entities:**
- Floor: Brown tile surfaces that form the walkable ground throughout the level
- Knight: The player's character that can move around and interact with objects
- Box: A movable brown block that can be pushed by the knight and falls into holes
- Wall: Gray stone barriers that block movement and have connecting edges between adjacent wall pieces
- Hole: Dark blue pits in the floor that destroy boxes that fall into them

**Interactions:**
- When the knight tries to move into a wall, the knight is prevented from moving and stays in its current position
- When the knight moves into a box, the box is pushed forward in the direction the knight was moving
- When a box is pushed against a wall or another box, both the box and the knight return to their previous positions
- When a box moves into a hole, the box disappears and the player scores one point

**Win condition:**
The player wins when all boxes have been removed from the level (by pushing them into holes).

**Lose condition:**
There is no way to lose this game.

**Objective:**
Push all the boxes into holes to clear them from the level while navigating around walls and tight spaces.
