**Player role:**
The player controls a rat that moves in four directions using arrow keys or WASD. The rat moves one space at a time on a grid-based floor.

**Entities:**
- A rat (the player character) that can move around the level
- Brown floor tiles that form the walkable surface throughout the level
- Movable blocks that can be pushed around by the player
- Green cheese that serves as the exit point
- Dark blue holes in the floor that act as traps
- Dirt walls that form barriers and cannot be moved through

**Interactions:**
- When the rat tries to move into a wall, it is blocked and stays in its current position
- When the rat pushes against a block, the block slides forward one space in the direction of the push
- When a block is pushed against a wall, exit, or another block, the push is cancelled and nothing moves
- When the rat falls into a hole, the rat is destroyed and the player loses a point
- When the rat reaches the cheese exit, the rat disappears and the player gains a point
- When a block is pushed into a hole, the block disappears

**Win condition:**
The player wins when all cheese exits have been collected (no cheese remains on the level).

**Lose condition:**
The player loses if the rat is destroyed by falling into a hole, or if 1000 time units pass without completing the level.

**Objective:**
Guide the rat to collect all the cheese while avoiding holes and using movable blocks strategically to reach the exits safely.
