**Player role:**
The player controls an avatar that can move in all directions and shoot a sword-like tool. The avatar can dig through dirt by walking into it and can shoot the sword tool to dig dirt from a distance.

**Entities:**
- Avatar: The player character that can move freely and shoot a digging tool
- Sword: A gray pickaxe-like tool that the avatar shoots to dig dirt remotely, appears briefly when used
- Wall: Immovable brown stone barriers that block movement
- Dirt: Brown dirt blocks that can be dug through by the avatar or the sword tool
- Exit door: A green door that serves as the level exit
- Diamond: Yellow gems that the player can collect for points, they shrink when collected
- Boulder: Gray rocks that constantly fall downward at a slow speed
- Crab: Red scorpion-like enemies that move randomly around the level
- Butterfly: Pink bat-like enemies that move randomly around the level

**Interactions:**
- When the avatar touches dirt, the dirt is destroyed
- When the sword touches dirt, the dirt is destroyed
- When the avatar touches a diamond, the player gains 5 points and the diamond is collected
- When any moving object (avatar or enemies) touches a wall, they are pushed back and cannot pass through
- When any moving object touches a boulder, they are pushed back and cannot pass through
- When the avatar touches a boulder from above, the boulder is destroyed
- When the avatar touches a butterfly, the butterfly is destroyed
- When the avatar touches a crab, the crab is destroyed
- When a boulder touches dirt, wall, diamond, or another boulder, it is pushed back and cannot pass through
- When enemies touch dirt or diamonds, they are pushed back and cannot pass through
- When a crab touches a butterfly, the butterfly is destroyed
- When a butterfly touches a crab, the butterfly turns into a diamond and the player gains 1 point
- When the avatar touches the exit door while carrying 10 or more diamonds, the exit door is destroyed (triggering the win condition)

**Win condition:**
The player wins when the exit door is destroyed, which happens when the avatar reaches it while carrying at least 10 diamonds.

**Lose condition:**
The player loses if the avatar is destroyed.

**Objective:**
Collect at least 10 diamonds by digging through dirt and navigating around falling boulders and enemies, then reach the green exit door to win the level.
