Player role:
The player controls an angel character that can move in any direction (up, down, left, right) around the game area.

Entities:
- An angel character that the player directly controls and can move freely
- Orange floor tiles that serve as targets to be stepped on
- Green floor tiles that appear after the player has stepped on orange tiles
- Rock walls that block movement and form boundaries
- A flickering indicator that appears briefly on green tiles when the player steps on them

Interactions:
- When the angel touches the edge of the screen, rock walls, or green floor tiles, it is pushed back to its previous position and cannot move through them
- When the angel steps on an orange floor tile, the tile transforms into a green floor tile and the player scores 1 point
- When the angel steps on a green floor tile, a flickering indicator briefly appears on that tile

Win condition:
The player wins when there are no orange floor tiles remaining on the screen (all have been turned green by stepping on them).

Lose condition:
The player loses if the angel character is somehow removed from the game.

Objective:
Step on every orange floor tile to turn them all green while navigating around the rock walls that block your path.
