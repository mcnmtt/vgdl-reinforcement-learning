Player role:
The player controls a rogue character that can move in all directions and shoot shovels. The avatar can shoot up to 2 shovels that flicker and have limited uses.

Entities:
- Brown floor tiles that serve as the basic ground surface
- Brown marsh areas that are stationary obstacles
- Red eaten brains that appear when zombies reach certain locations
- Gray tombstones that are smaller than normal size and block movement
- Green bush-like plants that occasionally shoot peas to the right with a small chance every few moments, and have a brief pause between shots
- Brown zombies that move slowly to the left and very rarely shoot red axes in the same direction
- Red axes that fly to the left at medium speed and are smaller than normal size
- Green peas that fly to the right at medium speed and are much smaller than normal size
- A green goal that looks like a cloak and serves as the exit
- Red spike-like portals that spawn zombies - some spawn zombies rarely, others spawn them very rarely
- Light gray shovels that flicker and can only be used twice, shot by the player
- Brown walls that block movement

Interactions:
- When a shovel hits marsh, the marsh transforms into a plant and the shovel is destroyed
- When a zombie reaches the goal, it transforms into eaten brains and the player loses a point
- When an axe hits a plant, the plant transforms back into marsh and the axe is destroyed
- When a pea hits a zombie, both are destroyed and the player gains a point
- When a pea hits a tombstone, the pea is destroyed
- When the avatar hits a wall, it is pushed back and cannot pass through
- When a zombie hits a wall, it is pushed back and cannot pass through
- When any projectile (axe or pea) reaches the edge of the screen, it is destroyed

Win condition:
The player wins if they survive for the full time limit without letting any zombies reach the goal.

Lose condition:
The player loses if their avatar is destroyed or if any zombie reaches the goal (creating eaten brains).

Objective:
Transform marsh areas into defensive plants using your shovel, then protect the goal from zombies by having the plants shoot them down before they can reach the exit.
