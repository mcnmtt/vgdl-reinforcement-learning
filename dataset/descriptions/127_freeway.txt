**Player role:**
The player controls a character that can move in all directions using keyboard input. The character has 5 health points and can switch between two states: a normal moving form and a dead ghost form that automatically drifts downward.

**Entities:**
- Floor tiles that serve as the basic walkable surface of the highway
- Street crossing areas that look different from regular floor
- A green exit portal that appears randomly with low probability every 16 game steps
- Orange trucks that move rightward at high speed
- Red trucks that move rightward at moderate speed  
- Orange trucks that move leftward at high speed
- Red trucks that move leftward at moderate speed
- Trees that act as solid obstacles
- Walls that can revive the player's character
- An invisible starting position marker
- An invisible spawn point that creates the exit portal

**Interactions:**
- When the moving character touches the goal portal, the character is removed and the player gains 10 points
- When the moving character reaches the goal portal, the character is transported to the exit
- When the moving character hits a tree, the character is pushed back to the previous position
- When the moving character is hit by any truck, the character loses health points and transforms into the dead ghost form
- When the dead ghost touches a wall, it transforms back into the normal moving character
- When the moving character hits a wall, the character is pushed back to the previous position
- When trucks reach the edge of the screen, they wrap around to the opposite side

**Win condition:**
The player wins if they survive for 1900 time steps (the timeout limit) or successfully reach the exit portal.

**Lose condition:**
The player loses if their character's health reaches zero and there are no avatar sprites remaining on the screen.

**Objective:**
Navigate your character across the dangerous highway filled with fast-moving trucks to reach the green exit portal while avoiding obstacles and managing your limited health points.
