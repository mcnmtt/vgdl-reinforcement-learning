**Player role:**
The player controls a human character armed with a shotgun that can shoot projectiles. The character moves around the game area and cannot pass through walls or move beyond the edge of the screen.

**Entities:**
- Floor: Black background tiles that form the ground of the game area
- City: Green buildings that serve as targets to be protected
- Bullet: Small flickering projectiles that shrink over time and disappear after a short duration when fired by the player
- Player character: A human with a shotgun that can move around and shoot bullets
- Slow enemy: An orange alien with a shotgun that moves slowly toward cities to destroy them
- Fast enemy: A yellow alien with a shotgun that moves quickly toward cities to destroy them  
- Nice guy: An orange friendly character that moves toward cities but helps protect them instead of destroying them
- Invisible spawn points: Hidden locations that periodically create new enemies or nice guys (each spawn point creates exactly 2 characters with delays between spawns)
- Wall: Solid gray blocks that block movement
- Death marker: An invisible tracker that appears when a nice guy is accidentally shot

**Interactions:**
- When any moving character reaches the edge of the screen or hits a wall, it bounces back and cannot pass through
- When an enemy reaches a city, both the enemy and city are destroyed and the player loses 1 point
- When a bullet hits a nice guy, the nice guy dies and transforms into a death marker, causing the player to lose 1 point
- When a bullet hits an enemy, the enemy is destroyed and the player gains 2 points
- When a nice guy reaches a city, the nice guy disappears and the player gains 2 points

**Win condition:**
The player wins when all enemies and nice guys are gone from the game area AND all spawn points have finished creating their characters (no more characters will appear).

**Lose condition:**
The player loses if all cities are destroyed OR if the player accidentally shoots a nice guy.

**Objective:**
Protect the cities by shooting the enemies while avoiding shooting the friendly nice guys who are also trying to help save the cities.
