Player role:
The player controls a girl character that can move in all directions using keyboard input. The avatar cannot move through walls or past the edges of the screen - it stops when hitting these boundaries.

Entities:
- Sky: Blue background tiles that fill empty spaces
- Walls: Brown wall segments that form barriers and automatically connect to adjacent walls
- Tree trunks: Brown immovable obstacles that block movement
- Cursed people: Yellow cursed men who remain stationary once created
- Demons: Red devil sprites that move around at moderate speed, occasionally shooting red fire bolts downward
- Angels: White angel sprites that move around slightly faster than demons, occasionally shooting ice salvation projectiles downward
- Citizens: Regular people who actively chase and move toward the player's avatar at moderate speed
- Blessed people: Holy figures who chase and move toward the player's avatar at moderate speed
- Input door: An open doorway that periodically spawns new citizens
- Output door: An open doorway that serves as an exit point
- Boxes: Gray cloud-like objects that can be pushed around
- Fire bolts: Red downward-moving projectiles shot by demons
- Salvation projectiles: Ice-colored downward-moving projectiles shot by angels

Interactions:
- When any moving character hits a wall, they reverse direction and step backward
- When the avatar hits a wall or screen edge, it steps backward
- When the avatar touches a box, the box is pushed forward in the direction of contact
- When boxes collide with each other, they bounce forward repeatedly
- When a box hits a wall, the box is destroyed
- When a fire bolt hits a wall, the bolt is destroyed and the score decreases by 1
- When a fire bolt hits a box, the bolt is destroyed and the score increases by 1
- When a fire bolt hits a citizen, the citizen transforms into a cursed person, the bolt is destroyed, and the score decreases by 1
- When a fire bolt hits the avatar, the bolt is destroyed and the score decreases by 5
- When a salvation projectile hits a wall, the projectile is destroyed and the score increases by 1
- When a salvation projectile hits a box, the projectile is destroyed and the score decreases by 1
- When a salvation projectile hits a citizen, the citizen transforms into a blessed person, the projectile is destroyed, and the score increases by 100
- When a citizen reaches the output door, the citizen disappears
- When a blessed person reaches the output door, the blessed person disappears

Win condition:
The player wins if they survive for the full time limit without any citizens being cursed.

Lose condition:
The player loses immediately if even one cursed person is created.

Objective:
Guide citizens safely to the exit door while preventing demons from cursing them with fire bolts, and try to have angels bless citizens with salvation projectiles for bonus points.
