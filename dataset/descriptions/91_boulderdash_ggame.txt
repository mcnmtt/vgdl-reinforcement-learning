**Player role:**
The player controls an avatar that can move around and shoot a sword-like weapon (pickaxe). The avatar can shoot this weapon to attack enemies and interact with the environment.

**Entities:**
- Avatar: A character that the player controls, capable of moving and shooting a pickaxe weapon
- Pickaxe: A flickering weapon that the avatar shoots, appears briefly when fired
- Boulder: A gray rock that moves downward slowly, acting as a falling obstacle
- Crab: A red scorpion-like enemy that moves randomly around the level
- Butterfly: A pink bat-like enemy that moves randomly around the level
- Diamond: A yellow collectible resource that appears smaller than normal size
- Dirt: Brown immovable ground tiles that form the terrain
- Wall: Immovable stone barriers that block movement
- Exit door: A green door that serves as the level exit
- Background: Hidden black tiles that fill empty spaces

**Interactions:**
- When the avatar touches a diamond, the diamond is collected as a resource
- When the avatar touches a crab, the avatar is destroyed
- When the avatar touches a butterfly, the avatar is destroyed
- When the avatar touches the exit door, the avatar is destroyed
- When a boulder hits the avatar, the avatar is destroyed and the score increases by 1
- When the avatar hits a wall, the avatar is pushed back
- When the pickaxe hits a wall, the pickaxe reverses direction
- When a boulder hits a wall, the boulder reverses direction
- When a crab hits a wall, the crab wraps around to the opposite side of the screen
- When a butterfly hits a wall, the butterfly wraps around to the opposite side of the screen
- When a crab touches the pickaxe, the crab is destroyed and the score increases by 1
- When the pickaxe touches a crab, the pickaxe is destroyed
- When a butterfly touches the pickaxe, the butterfly is destroyed and the score increases by 1
- When the pickaxe touches a butterfly, the pickaxe is destroyed

**Win condition:**
The player wins when there are no exit doors remaining in the level.

**Lose condition:**
The player loses when the avatar is destroyed.

**Objective:**
Navigate through the level while avoiding falling boulders and dangerous creatures, use your pickaxe to defeat enemies, and reach the exit door to complete the level.
