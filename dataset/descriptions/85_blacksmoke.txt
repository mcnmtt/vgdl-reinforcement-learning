**Player role:**
The player controls a character that can move in four directions and shoot shovels as projectiles. The character moves normally without any special physics like gravity or inertia.

**Entities:**
- A player character that can move around and shoot shovels
- Ground tiles that form the basic walkable surface, appearing brown
- Death tiles that are deadly gray areas scattered throughout the level  
- Walls made of dirt that block movement and automatically connect to adjacent walls
- Immovable boxes that block movement
- Keys that can be collected (only one can be held at a time)
- White locks that can be unlocked with keys
- An exit door that ends the level
- Smoke spawners that periodically create invisible orange projectiles that move in random directions
- Shovels that appear as flickering gray pickaxes when shot by the player

**Interactions:**
- When the player touches a wall, they are pushed back and cannot pass through
- When the player is on ground tiles, they are protected from death effects caused by smoke spawners
- When the player touches death tiles, they are destroyed
- When the player reaches the exit, they are removed from the level and gain a point
- When smoke spawner projectiles hit ground tiles, they transform into visible smoke spawners
- When smoke spawner projectiles hit ground tiles or walls, the projectiles are destroyed
- When the player touches a key, they collect it as a resource
- When the player touches a lock while carrying a key, the lock is destroyed and the player loses their key
- When the player tries to move toward a lock without a key, they are pushed back
- When a shovel hits a box, the box is destroyed and the player gains a point
- When smoke spawner projectiles hit boxes, the boxes are destroyed

**Win condition:**
The player wins when there are no more exits remaining (meaning they successfully reached the exit).

**Lose condition:**
The player loses when their character is destroyed (typically by touching death tiles).

**Objective:**
Navigate through the level while avoiding deadly areas, collect keys to unlock barriers, destroy boxes with your shovel for points, and reach the exit safely.
