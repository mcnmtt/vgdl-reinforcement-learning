**Player role:**
The player controls a character that can move in four directions (up, down, left, right) using keyboard input. The character moves one space at a time and can push certain objects.

**Entities:**
- Wall: A solid barrier that blocks movement and appears as the border and some internal structures of the level
- Exit door: A special location that the player must reach, appearing in two spots in the level
- Player character: The controllable sprite that the player moves around the level
- Boulder: A heavy object that can be pushed by the player character and rolls or slides when pushed
- Diamond: A collectible item that the player must gather

**Interactions:**
- When the player character moves into a wall, movement is blocked
- When the player character moves into a boulder, the boulder is pushed in the same direction if there's empty space behind it
- When the player character moves into a diamond, the diamond is collected and disappears
- When the player character reaches an exit door after collecting all diamonds, the level is completed
- Boulders cannot move through walls or other boulders

**Win condition:**
The player wins by collecting all diamonds in the level and then reaching one of the exit doors.

**Lose condition:**
There is no explicit lose condition - the player can continue attempting the puzzle indefinitely.

**Objective:**
Navigate through the level, push boulders out of the way as needed, collect all the diamonds, and reach an exit door to complete the level.
