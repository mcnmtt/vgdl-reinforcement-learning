Player role:
The player controls an avatar that moves with inertia, meaning it continues sliding in the direction of movement and requires input to change direction or stop.

Entities:
- Avatar: The player-controlled character that moves with inertia physics
- Blue pads: Stationary blue objects that remain fixed in place throughout the game
- Bullets: Objects that move randomly with inertial movement, bouncing around the play area

Interactions:
- When the avatar or any bullet hits a wall, it bounces off and continues moving in the opposite direction
- When the avatar touches a bullet, the bullet is destroyed
- When the avatar touches a blue pad, the blue pad is destroyed

Win condition:
The player wins when all blue pads have been destroyed.

Lose condition:
The player loses if the avatar is destroyed.

Objective:
Navigate the inertial avatar through the area to touch and destroy all blue pads while avoiding getting destroyed, managing the sliding physics that make precise movement challenging.
