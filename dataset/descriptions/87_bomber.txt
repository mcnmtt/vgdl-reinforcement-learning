**Player role:**
The player controls an avatar that can move in four directions and shoot bombs. The avatar has 10 health points and will be destroyed if health reaches zero.

**Entities:**
- Ground: Hidden floor tiles that provide a base surface
- Water: Blue water tiles that are deadly to touch
- Mud: Brown muddy ground created when water is transformed
- Box: Immovable brown blocks that can be destroyed
- Wall: Gray stone walls that block movement and projectiles
- Goal: A green open door that ends the level when reached
- Laser cannon (up-facing): Spawns upward-moving yellow lasers
- Laser cannon (right-facing): Spawns rightward-moving yellow lasers  
- Laser cannon (down-facing): Spawns downward-moving yellow lasers
- Laser cannon (left-facing): Spawns leftward-moving yellow lasers
- Yellow laser beams: Move slowly in their designated direction and destroy objects they hit
- Bomb (item): A bomb that the player can pick up and shoot - only one exists in the level
- Bomb (projectile): The moving bomb after being shot by the player
- Spark: A small spawning point created when bombs explode against solid objects
- Fire start: Orange spreading fire that expands to adjacent areas with certainty
- Fire end: Stationary orange fire created by the spreading fire

**Interactions:**
- When the avatar touches walls or boxes, it is pushed back and cannot pass through
- When the avatar touches water, the avatar is destroyed
- When the avatar reaches the goal, the goal is removed (winning the level)
- When the avatar touches a bomb item, the bomb transforms into a moving projectile
- When a bomb item touches the avatar, boxes, or water, the bomb item is destroyed
- When a moving bomb touches the avatar or lasers, the bomb changes direction toward them
- When a moving bomb hits walls, boxes, or laser cannons, it transforms into a spark
- When lasers hit boxes, the edge of the screen, the goal, or walls, the laser is destroyed
- When boxes touch fire, the box is destroyed and the player gains 1 point
- When water touches fire, the water transforms into mud
- When bomb items touch fire, they transform into sparks
- When fire touches boxes, the fire transforms into sparks
- When fire touches walls, water, or bomb items, the fire is destroyed
- When the avatar touches fire, the avatar loses health points
- When stationary fire touches ground or mud, the fire is destroyed

**Win condition:**
The player wins when the goal door is removed (by touching it).

**Lose condition:**
The player loses if the avatar is destroyed (health reaches zero) or if 1000 time units pass without completing the level.

**Objective:**
Navigate through a dangerous environment filled with water, laser cannons, and spreading fire to reach the green door. Use bombs strategically to clear destructible boxes while avoiding deadly hazards.
