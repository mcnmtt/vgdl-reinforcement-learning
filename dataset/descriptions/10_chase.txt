**Player role:**
The player controls a character that can move in all directions using keyboard input. The character cannot pass through solid obstacles and will be pushed back when trying to move into them.

**Entities:**
- Floor: Hidden grass tiles that form the ground of the game world
- Character: The player's character, depicted as a princess figure, that moves around the game area
- Wall: Immovable tree obstacles that block movement for all characters
- Scared goat: A blue bird-like creature that actively flees away from the player's character
- Angry goat: An orange bird-like creature that actively chases and pursues the player's character
- Carcass: A brown, smaller worm-like remains that appear when scared goats are caught

**Interactions:**
- When any goat touches a wall, the goat is pushed back and cannot pass through
- When the player's character touches a wall, the character is pushed back and cannot pass through  
- When the player's character touches an angry goat, the character is destroyed and the player loses 1 point
- When a scared goat touches the player's character, the scared goat transforms into a carcass and the player gains 1 point
- When a scared goat touches a carcass, the carcass transforms into an angry goat
- When a carcass touches a scared goat, the scared goat is destroyed

**Win condition:**
The player wins when there are no scared goats remaining on the screen.

**Lose condition:**
The player loses when their character is destroyed by touching an angry goat.

**Objective:**
Hunt down all the scared goats by touching them to turn them into carcasses, while avoiding the angry goats that will destroy you on contact.
