Player role:
The player controls a character that can move in all directions and shoot projectiles upward. The character carries explosive ammunition and can only shoot when they have at least 5 units of ammunition, with each shot consuming 5 units.

Entities:
- A brown tree trunk that remains stationary and blocks movement
- Yellow broken eggshells that remain stationary after being created
- Golden explosive ammunition that the player can collect, with a maximum capacity of 20 units
- Fast chickens that move horizontally at high speed, change direction when hitting walls, and drop eggs downward frequently with short intervals between drops
- Slow chickens that move horizontally at low speed, change direction when hitting walls, and drop eggs downward less frequently with longer intervals between drops
- Blue projectiles that the player shoots upward and disappear when reaching the edge of the screen
- Red eggs that fall downward slowly from chickens
- Walls that block movement for all characters

Interactions:
- When chickens hit walls, they reverse direction and step back slightly
- When the player hits walls, they step back slightly and cannot pass through
- When eggs hit walls, they transform into broken eggshells
- When the player touches an egg, they gain 1 unit of explosive ammunition, earn 1 point, and the egg disappears
- When a blue projectile hits a chicken, the chicken is destroyed and the player earns 100 points
- When blue projectiles reach the edge of the screen, they disappear

Win condition:
The player wins either by destroying all chickens or by surviving until the time limit of 1500 time units is reached.

Lose condition:
The player loses if there is at least 1 broken eggshell on the screen.

Objective:
Shoot all the chickens while avoiding letting any eggs hit the walls and break, as broken eggs will cause you to lose the game.
