Player role:
- The player controls the avatar.

Entities:
- There are two entities: pad (colored BLUE) and avatar (InertialAvatar with GravityPhysics).

Interactions:
- The avatar can be killed by colliding with walls or the EOS entity.
- The avatar can also be killed if it moves too slowly relative to the pad.

Win condition:
- The game is won when the player reaches a count of 4 pads.

Lose condition:
- The game is lost if the player's avatar collides with walls or the EOS entity, or if it moves too slowly relative to the pad.

Objective:
- The objective is to reach a count of 4 pads without being killed by walls or the EOS entity, or by moving too slowly.
