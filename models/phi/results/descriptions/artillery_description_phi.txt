Player role:
- The player controls the avatar, which is an aimed bullet.

Entities:
- There are two entities: a pad (immovable, color blue) and an avatar (aimed bullet).

Interactions:
- The avatar can kill bullets and walls.
- Bullets can kill walls and the avatar.
- The avatar can step back when hitting a wall or reaching the end of the level (EOS).
- The pad can kill bullets and the avatar.

Win condition:
- The player wins when the count of pads reaches a certain threshold (win=True).

Lose condition:
- The player loses when the avatar is killed by a bullet or wall (win=False).

Objective:
- The objective is to increase the count of pads by avoiding being killed by bullets or walls.
