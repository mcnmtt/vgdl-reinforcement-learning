Player role: 
The player controls an avatar that can shoot bullets.

Entities: 
The game features a blue immovable pad, an avatar, bullets, and walls.

Interactions: 
The player can shoot bullets that can kill enemies (pad, bullet, and avatar) and interact with walls. The avatar can step back when it hits a wall.

Win condition: 
The player wins by killing all enemies (pad, bullet, and avatar).

Lose condition: 
The player loses when the avatar hits the pad.

Objective: 
The objective of the game is to survive by killing all enemies.
