Player role:
The player controls an avatar that moves under gravity using inertial movement.

Entities:
The game contains pad objects and walls that act as obstacles within the level.

Interactions:
The avatar is destroyed if it collides with walls or reaches the edge of the screen. When the avatar collides with a pad while moving slowly relative to it, the pad is removed.

Win condition:
The player wins when the number of pad objects reaches the specified limit of four.

Lose condition:
The player loses if the avatar is destroyed.

Objective:
Move the avatar carefully to interact with pads while avoiding collisions with walls or the edge of the screen.
